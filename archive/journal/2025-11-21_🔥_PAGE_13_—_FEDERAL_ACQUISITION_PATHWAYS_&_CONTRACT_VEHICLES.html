2. Federal Contract Vehicles (Entry-Level)


🇺🇸 These are the fastest ways for NI-AI to start receiving federal payments.


Micro-Purchase Threshold (MPT)

• Any agency can buy NI-AI without competition up to $10,000.

• Perfect for demos, pilots, or a single-license EchoAuth deployment.


Simplified Acquisition Threshold (SAT)

• Up to $250,000 with limited competition.

• Ideal for NI-AI pilot programs installed into a federal clinic, school, or cybersecurity unit.


Purchase Cards (GPC)

• Government cardholders can pay instantly.

• NI-AI can be purchased like a SaaS subscription under this channel.
