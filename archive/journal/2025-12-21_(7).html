SCHOOL PORTAL — OBSERVATION & EDUCATIONAL SUPPORT


Purpose (professional, plain)


The School Portal exists to allow educators to observe, document, and support the child’s development in an educational setting without overriding parental authority or turning the system into a compliance tool.


This portal connects school-day reality to the child’s broader support system.


⸻


1️⃣ Observation & Documentation


What school staff can do

• Log classroom observations

• Record routines, engagement, transitions

• Note challenges or successes factually

• Add time-stamped entries to the shared record


What this means

• Observations are visible to parents (as allowed)

• No interpretation is enforced

• No labeling or scoring


Professional framing:

Schools contribute observations, not conclusions.


⸻


2️⃣ Educational Support Proposals


What school staff can do

• Propose supports or accommodations

• Suggest routines, tools, or adjustments

• Reference IEP-related needs (documents only)


What they cannot do

• Activate supports

• Change child programs

• Enforce decisions outside school scope


Professional framing:

Schools may recommend, parents decide.


⸻


3️⃣ Execution of Approved Supports


What schools can execute

• Parent-approved supports

• Approved routines or tools

• Approved child programs during school hours


Boundaries

• Execution only within approved scope

• No deviation without parent confirmation


Professional framing:

Schools implement, they do not control.


⸻


4️⃣ Outcome Logging


What is logged

• What support was used

• When it was used

• Observed outcome (factual, not interpretive)


Why this matters

• Parents see what works at school

• Continuity across home and school

• Reduced repetition and meetings


Professional framing:

Outcomes are logged as events, not evaluations.


⸻


5️⃣ Document Uploads


What can be uploaded

• IEPs

• Classroom notes

• Educational plans

• Progress summaries (non-clinical)


Governance

• Parent controls visibility

• Documents are reference-only


Professional framing:

Documents support coordination, not enforcement.


⸻


6️⃣ Visibility & Privacy Limits


What schools can see

• Only school-relevant data

• Only what parents allow

• No full medical or home logs by default


What schools cannot see

• Private family discussions

• Caregiver-only logs

• Unapproved sensor data


Professional framing:

Access is role-bounded and consent-based.


⸻


7️⃣ What the School Portal Explicitly Does NOT Do

• ❌ Make decisions for the child

• ❌ Diagnose or assess clinically

• ❌ Control caregivers

• ❌ Override parents

• ❌ Enforce behavior grading through the system


Professional framing:

The School Portal supports education, not authority transfer.


⸻


8️⃣ How This Helps the Child

• School routines align with home routines

• Fewer conflicting expectations

• Reduced stress from inconsistency

• Better transitions between environments


The child experiences continuity, not fragmentation.


⸻
