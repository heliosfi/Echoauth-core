• blank staring

• slowed responses

• posture collapse

• soft “shutdown breathing”

• closed body posture

• stillness instead of movement

• quiet withdrawal

• reduced eye scanning

• minimal gestures

• soft humming

“Silent distress detected — check sensory and emotional load.”
