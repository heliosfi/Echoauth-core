1. Executive Summary (CDFI-Ready Tone)


EchoAuth™ is a safety-driven biometric authentication and communication system designed for non-verbal autistic children, their parents, and their caregivers. It verifies who is interacting with the child, prevents dangerous miscommunication, and provides real-time emotional-state alerts using AI + coherence checks.


The goal is simple:

Give ASD families a tool that makes care safer, calmer, and more connected.


This loan will directly fund:

• MVP completion (backend + EchoAuth Chamber)

• Hardware modules for authentication testing

• Safety compliance & licensing

• Pilot programs with local daycares, schools, and in-home caregivers

• Affordable access programs for low-income ASD families


CDFI funding will help us finalize the version required for early government and institutional partnerships.


⸻


2. Problem Being Solved


Millions of autistic children—especially non-verbal children—cannot clearly express:

• Who picked them up

• Whether they feel safe

• If their caregiver is following protocol

• If they are distressed

• If an adult is misrepresenting themselves


This creates daily risk for families, many of whom already struggle financially and emotionally.


Parents want reliable safety, caregivers want clear guidance, and institutions need tools that meet compliance standards.


EchoAuth solves this with:

• Coherence-based identity verification

• Emotional-state sensing

• Caregiver protocol guidance

• Session-level safety logs

• Behavior-support prompts


This improves child safety, caregiver consistency, and parent peace of mind.


⸻


3. EchoAuth Payment Structure (Corrected & Final)


To avoid over-burdening low-income families, EchoAuth uses a hybrid, fair-access model:


A. Parents / Families

• Families who can afford it: Standard subscription

• Low-income ASD households: Affordable Access Program (reduced rate)

• Wearing-energy donation fund: Future wearables will generate micro-donations to subsidize free or reduced access for struggling families.


B. Individual Caregivers (Not Institutions)

• ONE-TIME onboarding fee only

Caregivers do not carry recurring subscription costs.


C. Institutions

• Daycares

• Schools

• Clinical care centers


These pay standard subscription-based licensing, which is tax-deductible as a safety and support expense.


This model:

• Protects struggling families

• Keeps caregiver costs low

• Leverages institutions to create sustainability

• Ensures EchoAuth is accessible across income levels
