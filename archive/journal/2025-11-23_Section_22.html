⭐ 22.5 Multi-Engine Coordination


The Home Hub integrates the following engines:


✔ SODE (Sensory Overload Detection)


Detects harmful sensory spikes.


✔ BPE (Behavior Prediction Engine)


Detects pacing → running → elopement phases.


✔ ESRE (Emotion Engine)


Identifies emotional changes from posture/movement.


✔ MEWE (Medical Early Warning)


Detects collapse, breath irregularity, sickness onset.


✔ ERDE (Environment Risk Engine)


Maps home hazards and real-time danger.


✔ CPIE (Child Intelligence Engine)


Uses historical data to adapt accuracy.


✔ CAE (Communication Engine)


Translates nonverbal cues → messages.
