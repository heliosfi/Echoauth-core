Subject: Loan Application — Assistive Technology & Secure Caregiver Infrastructure (NYC)


Dear Loan Committee,


My name is Nicholas B. Carty, Founder of Helios F.I LLC, a New York–based technology company developing EchoAuth, a caregiver-mediated, non-verbal–safe authentication and interaction system designed for autistic children and neurodivergent individuals.


EchoAuth addresses a documented gap in current systems: most digital tools require verbal confirmation, interpretation of behavior, or escalation under uncertainty. EchoAuth removes those pressures by design. The system supports non-verbal interaction, defaults to non-action under uncertainty, and preserves human oversight at all times.


We are seeking CDFI financing to complete final deployment readiness, pilot implementation, and institutional onboarding across NYC-area schools, clinics, and caregiver organizations.


Loan proceeds will be used for:

• Final engineering and system hardening

• Secure infrastructure and compliance costs

• Pilot deployment preparation

• Business operations and early customer acquisition


Helios F.I LLC is positioned for:

• Institutional subscription revenue

• Government and healthcare pilots

• Long-term scalability without mission drift


We are committed to repayment through contracted institutional revenue and structured subscription models.
