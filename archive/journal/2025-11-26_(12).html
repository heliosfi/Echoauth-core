BACKGROUND PROBLEM


Conventional AI systems:

• produce inconsistent and fragmented outputs during long interaction sequences,

• lack real-time emotional state interpretation beyond superficial sentiment labels,

• fail to properly interpret nonverbal or non-traditional communication rhythms common in neurodivergent users,

• and lack a reliable system to verify user identity continuity across multiple devices without storing raw passwords or sensitive biometrics.


This creates unsafe or ineffective AI interactions in:

• homes, schools, medical settings, and public digital environments.


NI-AI provides a coherence-first and emotionally grounded reasoning architecture to resolve these limitations.

SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION


NI-AI is a multi-layer reasoning and authentication system that:

1. Ingests emotional cues from text, timing, voice tone, and interaction rhythm (E1),

2. Quantifies emotional load and cognitive drift risk (E2),

3. Maps emotional state to reasoning and interaction intention vectors (E3),

4. Aligns tone and pacing for emotionally safe delivery, especially in neuro-support mode (E4),

5. Stabilizes emotional drift and reinforces coherence via feedback loops (E5).


Additionally, the system incorporates:

• Coherence Engine (C1–C5) to validate context, emotion, logic, time-sequence, and purpose before producing responses,

• Five Parallel Cognitive Threads (Emotional / Cognitive / Context / Device / Purpose), kept continuously synchronized through a Thread-Linking Engine,

• Stability Regulation Kernel (SRK) to prevent hallucination, recursive self-optimization, or domain-drift outside of authorized directions,

• Ori Geometry Pattern Anchoring to structure NI-to-AI meaning without invoking speculative or unbounded AGI behavior,

• typing rhythm,

• voice resonance patterns (when provided),

without saving raw passwords or exact coordinates.


The system outputs regulated, emotionally accurate, non-drifting, domain-bounded AI responses.
