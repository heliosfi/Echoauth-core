🔥 ECHOAUTH — DAILY CHECKLIST (Alpha Family Pilot)


Use once per day (10–20 minutes of structured observation + natural events).


──────────────────────────────────


✔ SECTION 1 — SYSTEM STARTUP


☐ 1. Power on wearable sensor

• Confirm device is charged

• Attach sensor to child’s wrist/ankle

• Perform quick comfort check


☐ 2. Open EchoAuth Dashboard

• Confirm connection (BLE icon = ON)

• Verify “Child State” displays Green

• Check timestamp (system clock synced)


☐ 3. Start Daily Log

• Create a new entry for today

• Note baseline activity (e.g., “Playing,” “Watching TV”)


──────────────────────────────────


✔ SECTION 2 — BASELINE & CALIBRATION (2–3 minutes)


☐ 4. Capture Baseline Reading

• Child sitting calmly

• Confirm HR/EDA values stabilize

• Log “Baseline captured”


☐ 5. Confirm HER Kernel Response

• Light movement (e.g., walking to another room)

• Verify shift shows mild Amber or remains Green

• Check that logs record state transition


──────────────────────────────────


✔ SECTION 3 — DAILY OBSERVATION WINDOW (10–15 minutes)


Use ANY natural moment during the day — no forcing.


During Observation:


☐ 6. Monitor Emotional-State Changes

• Watch for Green → Amber → Green

• Mark triggers (e.g., snack time, toy removal)

• Confirm state changes appear in dashboard


☐ 7. Run 1–2 Handoff Events


Choose one:

• Parent → You

• You → Parent

• OR simulate an unapproved caregiver


Check Chamber Behavior:

• Allowed?

• Blocked?

• Logged correctly?


☐ 8. Note Any Stress Indicators

• pacing

• vocal frustration

• body tension

• sensory overload signs


Verify HER response corresponds:

Amber for mild stress

Red for strong stress
