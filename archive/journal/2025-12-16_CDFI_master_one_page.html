EchoAuth™ — CDFI Loan Committee Overview


Borrower: Nicholas B. Carty

Entity: HeliosFi LLC

Product Focus: Governance-first human–AI support system for caregiver-assisted workflows (including non-verbal and neurodivergent contexts)


⸻


Executive Summary


EchoAuth™ is a safety-governed human–AI support system designed for environments where trust, consent, and accountability are essential. The system prioritizes caregiver control and transparent decision boundaries, reducing risk commonly associated with autonomous or opaque AI tools.


The requested financing supports prototype refinement, pilot readiness, and compliance-aligned documentation. The project is structured for disciplined, incremental deployment rather than speculative scale.


⸻


Problem Statement


Caregivers and families often rely on fragmented tools that:

• Lack clear permission boundaries

• Are difficult to audit or pause

• Introduce risk when AI acts without explicit authorization

• Do not align with expectations around consent and accountability


These gaps create barriers to adoption in sensitive, human-centered settings.


⸻


Solution Overview


EchoAuth enforces a governance-first architecture:

• Human-initiated intent: All actions begin with a caregiver or authorized user.

• Bounded AI assistance: AI performs limited, supportive computation only.

• Governance layer (MCG): A mandatory approval checkpoint that can allow, pause, or block actions based on safety and consent rules.

• Mediator routing: Ensures no component bypasses governance.

• Transparent interface: Displays system state and decisions for auditability.


The system is designed to pause safely rather than proceed under uncertainty.


⸻


Risk Management & Controls

• No autonomous AI execution

• No direct user-to-AI bypass

• Explicit pause/lock capability

• Audit-ready decision flow

• Clear separation of roles (human, AI, governance)


This structure aligns with responsible deployment standards expected by mission-driven lenders.


⸻


Use of Funds (High-Level)


Loan proceeds will be used for:

• Prototype refinement and testing

• Caregiver-guided pilot preparation

• Compliance-aligned documentation

• Engineering validation and review


Funds are not allocated toward speculative expansion or large-scale automation.


⸻


Repayment Strategy


Repayment will be supported by:

• Existing consulting and technical services income

• Early pilot engagements and service-based partnerships

• Phased product development tied to measurable milestones


The repayment plan does not rely on large contracts or unproven growth assumptions.


⸻


Community & Mission Alignment


EchoAuth aligns with CDFI priorities by:

• Supporting families and caregivers

• Emphasizing safety and accountability

• Advancing responsible technology use

• Focusing on sustainable, community-centered impact


⸻


Conclusion


EchoAuth is designed to make AI safer and more accountable, not more autonomous.

The project emphasizes disciplined growth, transparent operations, and responsible stewardship of capital.
