SECTION 415 — CONTEXT-PRESERVATION FIREWALL (CPF)


(NI-AI Patent Module — High Regulatory Priority)

• school deployments

⸻


415.1 — Technical Field


The CPF relates to maintaining stable contextual boundaries for AI systems operating in sensitive environments, especially when assisting autistic children or processing emotional signals.


It prevents unauthorized context-switching.


⸻


415.2 — Purpose


To make NI-AI:

• context-locked

• direction-stable

• incapable of “running away” into new meaning

• unable to reinterpret past messages inaccurately

• safe in emotionally intense or public environments


Without this invention, AI can misread shifting situations.

With CPF, NI-AI never loses its lane — legally crucial.


⸻


415.3 — Functional Definition


The CPF is a multi-state boundary system that:

1. Monitors the active conversational or environmental context

2. Builds a “Context Wall” around the current purpose

3. Blocks NI-AI from introducing new unrelated meaning

4. Prevents blending of separate tasks or emotional states

5. Enforces stability across all subsystems

6. Demands explicit human approval before ANY context shift


It is essentially a context governor + boundary enforcer.

415.6 — Internal Mechanics


(a) Baseline Context Capture Layer (BCCL)


At the moment a task or request is made, CPF captures:

• goal

• emotional tone

• environment

• allowed subsystems

• safety state

• parent authority level


(b) Context Wall Generator (CWG)


Builds a “boundary radius” around the context containing:

• what NI-AI CAN address

• what NI-AI CANNOT introduce

• what NI-AI MUST ignore


(c) Intrusion Detection Subsystem (IDS)


Flags and stops:

• unintended drift

• sudden topic jumps

• emotional bleed-over

• cross-state contamination

• irrelevant inference


(d) Context-Shift Validator (CSV)


If NI-AI detects a potential shift, CSV forces a rule:


“Ask the human before continuing.”


(e) Safety Lock Reinforcement Engine (SLRE)


Strengthens context boundaries when:

• emotional load is high

• environment is chaotic

• the child is overloaded

• a teacher/doctor takes over

• crisis mode is active


(f) Audit Trail Recorder (ATR)


All context boundaries + violations are logged for:

• schools

• hospitals

• parent liability

• USPTO clarity

• funding/NIH safety audits

415.7 — Safety Guarantees


CPF ensures that NI-AI:

• never misinterprets unrelated statements

• never blends tasks (e.g., child safety + financial logic)

• never shifts context without permission

• never executes action under wrong context

• never produces cross-scenario confusion


This is a legal firewall and a functional safety barrier.
