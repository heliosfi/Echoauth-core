GRANT BUDGET LINE-ITEM MAPPING


EchoAuth · NI-AI · Safety-First Architecture


Budget Philosophy (State This Once)


Funds support availability, safety enforcement, and auditability — not usage, outcomes, or escalation.


This sentence belongs at the top of every budget narrative.


⸻


1️⃣ Personnel (Core — Allowed & Expected)


Purpose: Build and maintain safety, not features.

• Principal Investigator / Technical Lead

Architecture, authority separation, safety review

• Software Engineer(s)

State machines, authorization enforcement, logging

• Security / Privacy Consultant (part-time)

Default-deny posture, auditability

• QA / Validation Lead (part-time)

Failure testing, non-action validation


Reviewer cue: Personnel effort maps to risk reduction, not speed.


⸻


2️⃣ Software Development (Non-Feature)


Purpose: Deterministic control, not optimization.

• State discipline implementation (MCG)

• Authorization enforcement (EchoAuth)

• Read-only explanation layer (NI-AI)

• Interface contracts & validation

• Audit logging (append-only)


Explicit exclusion (say this):


No funds are allocated to predictive modeling, behavioral scoring, or autonomous optimization.


⸻


3️⃣ Security, Privacy & Compliance


Purpose: Regulator confidence.

• Threat modeling

• Privacy review

• Data minimization controls

• Retention & deletion workflows

• External audit prep


Reviewer cue: Proactive compliance lowers institutional risk.


⸻


4️⃣ Wearables (Limited & Bounded)


Purpose: Passive awareness only.

• Small batch devices for pilot

• Firmware restricted to sensing + reflection

• Replacement units (failure tolerance)


Explicit constraint:


Wearables have no authority, no triggers, and no control pathways.


⸻


5️⃣ Pilot Operations (Safety-First)


Purpose: Prove restraint works.

• Simulated / stubbed execution only

• Environment setup (zones)

• Observation tooling

• Incident logging & review


Success metric: Correct refusal to act.


⸻


6️⃣ Caregiver Onboarding (One-Time)


Purpose: Boundary alignment.

• Module development (finite)

• Orientation delivery

• Completion certification

• Materials (print / digital)


Important: No recurring training line items.


⸻


7️⃣ Family Access Support (Non-Outcome Based)


Purpose: Equity without pressure.

• Access administration

• Subsidy pool management

• Institutional coordination


Explicit rule:


Funding does not unlock features, urgency, or outcomes.


⸻


8️⃣ Infrastructure & Hosting


Purpose: Availability, not scale hype.

• Secure hosting

• Redundancy

• Backups

• Monitoring (non-behavioral)


⸻


9️⃣ Evaluation & Reporting


Purpose: Auditability.

• Log review

• Safety metrics

• Non-action reporting

• Grant reporting


Avoid: Engagement or activation metrics.


⸻


10️⃣ Indirect Costs (If Applicable)


Handled per agency rules. No special framing needed.


⸻


WHAT REVIEWERS LIKE TO SEE (USE THIS LANGUAGE)

• “Default-deny execution”

• “Human-in-the-loop with bounded authority”

• “Non-action treated as a successful outcome”

• “Deterministic, auditable behavior”

• “Data minimization by design”


⸻


WHAT TO AVOID (RED FLAGS)

• “Improve engagement”

• “Increase usage”

• “Optimize outcomes”

• “Predict behavior”

• “Adaptive personalization”


⸻


ONE-PARAGRAPH BUDGET JUSTIFICATION (DROP-IN)


Funds are allocated to implement and validate a safety-first control architecture that prevents action without explicit authorization and human oversight. Budget emphasis is placed on deterministic state discipline, authorization enforcement, audit logging, privacy safeguards, and finite onboarding. No funds support predictive modeling, behavioral inference, or usage-based optimization. Success is measured by correct refusal to act under uncertainty and complete auditability of system behavior.
