⭐ PAGE 30 — ASD EMOTIONAL LITERACY & EXPRESSION ENGINE (ELEE)


“How EchoAuth helps ASD children understand, identify, and express emotions safely — without overwhelm.”


──────────────────────────────────


The ASD emotional experience is not like neurotypical emotional processing.


Many ASD children experience:

• difficulty identifying emotions

• difficulty labeling emotions

• delayed emotional recognition

• emotional shutdown

• emotional overload

• monotone affect

• deep but internalized feelings

• struggles with expression

• confusion between physical sensations and emotions

• difficulty matching sensations to emotional names


ELEE is built to solve these challenges without pressure or emotional demand.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 30.1 — THE 3 CORE FUNCTIONS OF ELEE


⭐ Function 1 — Emotional Identification


EchoAuth helps the child understand:

• what emotion they’re feeling

• where it’s happening in the body

• how strong it is


ALWAYS done with low pressure and simple questions.


⸻


⭐ Function 2 — Emotional Expression Support


The child can express emotions in ways that feel safe:


✔ tapping an icon

✔ selecting a mood color

✔ choosing from simple words

✔ using gestures

✔ using visuals


This removes speech pressure.


⸻


⭐ Function 3 — Emotional Regulation Pathways


EchoAuth helps the child regulate by:

• slowing rhythm

• grounding

• simplifying environment

• shifting pacing

• providing comfort phrases

• reducing demands


ELEE stabilizes emotional intensity without dismissing feelings.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 30.2 — THE ELEE EMOTION MAP (ASD-Safe)


EchoAuth uses 6 primary emotional states, simplified for ASD neurology:

1. Calm

2. Focused

3. Stressed

4. Overwhelmed

5. Sad

6. Frustrated


This map is:


✔ literal

✔ predictable

✔ visually simple

✔ less mentally load

✔ stabilizing


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 30.3 — EMOTIONAL IDENTIFICATION PROCESS (EIP)


A 4-step ASD-safe process:


⭐ Step 1 — Sensation Check


“Is your body tight or loose?”

“Fast or slow?”


ASD users often feel emotions physically first.


⸻


⭐ Step 2 — Color Match


“Pick the color that fits.”

EchoAuth uses a 6-color system.


⸻


⭐ Step 3 — Name Choice


EchoAuth offers 2–4 emotional words to choose from.


Example:


Feeling tight → “Stressed or Overwhelmed?”

Feeling slow → “Sad or Tired?”


⸻


⭐ Step 4 — Intensity Dial


Child chooses:

• low

• medium

• high


Simple. Literal. Accessible.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 30.4 — EXPRESSION BRIDGES (EB)


ELEE supports expression without verbal pressure.


⭐ 1. Visual Expression


Tap icons representing emotions.


⭐ 2. Story Expression


Choose “what happened” from pictures.


⭐ 3. Gesture Expression


Point → nod → shake head.


⭐ 4. Micro-Choice Expression


“Do you want to share?”

Yes / No.


This is essential during shutdown risk.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 30.5 — REGULATION MODES (ASD-Safe)


ELEE engages one of 4 regulation modes:


⭐ Mode 1 — Soft Mode

• slow pacing

• gentle tone

• minimal wording


⭐ Mode 2 — Grounding Mode

• breath pacing

• sensory reset

• stability phrases


⭐ Mode 3 — Cognitive Mode


For children who regulate through logic:


“We do one step.”

“Slow and steady.”


⭐ Mode 4 — Escape Mode


Support child in safely exiting the environment.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 30.6 — ELEE PHRASES (ASD-Safe Language)


EchoAuth uses emotional language that reduces distress:


✔ “It’s okay.”

✔ “Feelings come and go.”

✔ “Slow is safe.”

✔ “You decide.”

✔ “I’m here.”


NEVER:


❌ “Calm down.”

❌ “Why are you upset?”

❌ “Use your words.”

❌ “Stop crying.”

❌ “It’s not a big deal.”


These phrases damage emotional safety.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 30.7 — EMOTIONAL RECOVERY PATHWAYS (ERP)


A structured 3-step formula:


⭐ Step 1 — Stabilize


Breath, grounding, pacing.


⭐ Step 2 — Understand


Emotion label, intensity, cause.


⭐ Step 3 — Integrate


Simple coping strategy:

• break

• quiet

• water

• one-step task

• caregiver support


Regulation is complete, not rushed.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 30.8 — CAREGIVER EMOTION COACHING


EchoAuth helps caregivers speak the right language:

• “Give space.”

• “Lower voice.”

• “Let them process.”

• “Be neutral.”

• “Do one step at a time.”


The caregiver learns ASD-safe emotion support.
