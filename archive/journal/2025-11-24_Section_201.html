🔥 201.1 — Clinical Interaction Boundary (CIB)


The CIB defines what NI-AI can AND cannot do in clinical or therapeutic contexts.


The boundary enforces:


✔ No diagnosis

✔ No medical treatment instructions

✔ No psychiatric labeling

✔ No medication recommendations

✔ No claims of medical accuracy


It allows:


• emotional stabilization

• sensory-guidance

• grounding techniques

• ASD-friendly communication modes

• pattern detection

• escalation warnings

• caregiver support


This is EXACTLY what’s required for ADA, HIPAA, and CLIA compliance.

🔥 201.2 — Therapist-In-The-Loop Protocol (TILP)


For clinical use, NI-AI must follow TILP:

• therapist or clinician ALWAYS remains the decision-maker

• NI-AI NEVER replaces a professional

• NI-AI provides only:

• emotional state maps

• pattern summaries

• drift alerts

• sensory risk indicators

• communication support

• all high-risk recommendations require clinician review


This makes NI-AI legally safe and clinically admissible.

🔥 201.4 — Cognitive Safety Thresholding Engine (CSTE)


This engine ensures NI-AI NEVER overwhelms a neurodivergent or clinical user.


It monitors:


• response density

• emotional pressure

• cognitive load

• pacing

• sensory overload patterns


If thresholds exceed safe limits:


NI-AI automatically:

• slows down

• reduces output

• switches tone

• breaks content into steps

• asks for confirmation

• initiates grounding protocol

• notifies caregiver (optional mode)


CSTE = legal protection + user safety.

🔥 201.5 — Clinical Escalation Interface (CEI)


If NI-AI detects:

• self-harm signals

• escalating panic

• extreme shutdown

• aggression

• autistic meltdown patterns

• dissociation signals


CEI activates.


CEI CAN:


✔ notify caregiver

✔ notify therapist (if connected)

✔ switch NI-AI into “Guardian Calm Mode”

✔ lock high-risk actions

✔ provide grounding strategies

✔ request immediate human contact


CEI CANNOT:


✘ call 911 directly

✘ assume medical authority

✘ override user consent outside Emergency Mode


This protects both YOU and NI-AI legally.
