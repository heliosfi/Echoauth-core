CDC Loan Officer Call Script (5 minutes)


1️⃣ Opening (20–30 seconds)


“Thanks for taking the time. I’m Nicholas B. Carty, Founder of HeliosFi LLC, with Monique King Carty as Co-Founder. We’re an early-stage, pre-revenue business preparing to complete a prototype, run controlled testing, and move into initial customer onboarding.”


Why this works: Clear stage, calm tone, no hype.


⸻


2️⃣ Why You’re Calling (30 seconds)


“I wanted to confirm program fit before submitting a formal application. We’re exploring CDC-aligned options, particularly SBA Community Advantage or a small business working-capital loan.”


Why: Signals discipline and respect for their process.


⸻


3️⃣ Use of Funds (60 seconds)


“Funds would be used strictly for non-real-estate working capital:

• Final product development and system readiness

• Controlled testing and pilot execution

• Compliance, accounting, and operational setup

• Early customer onboarding and market entry

• A modest working-capital reserve


No real estate, no speculative use.”


Why: Hits CDC risk screens directly.


⸻


4️⃣ Business Readiness & Repayment Path (60 seconds)


“We’re transitioning from build to validation. The goal of the loan is to complete testing and activate early customers. Repayment is expected to be supported by initial service revenue following pilot completion, with disciplined operating controls in place.”


Why: You’re not claiming revenue—you’re showing a path.


⸻


5️⃣ The Three Questions to Ask (this is key)


Ask only these:

1.


“Based on our stage and use of funds, does SBA Community Advantage appear to be an appropriate fit?”


2.


“Are there any pre-revenue considerations you’d want us to address before moving forward?”


3.


“What materials would you recommend preparing before submitting an application?”


Why: Lets them guide the process (they like that).


⸻


6️⃣ Close (20 seconds)


“I appreciate the guidance. We’re looking to move forward the right way and only apply once we’re aligned.”


Stop talking. Let them respond.


⸻


What NOT to Say


❌ “How fast can I get funded?”

❌ “I need X dollars urgently”

❌ “Other lenders said…”

❌ Over-explaining the tech


⸻


If they ask about credit


Say:


“Personal credit is strong and stable. We’re happy to discuss details if and when we move toward application.”


⸻


If they ask about amount


Say:


“We’re targeting an amount appropriate for prototype completion and early market readiness, and we’re open to guidance on sizing.”


⸻


If they say ‘come back when you have revenue’


Respond calmly:


“Understood. Is there a CDC-aligned option you typically recommend for businesses in the testing-to-revenue transition phase?”


This keeps doors open.
