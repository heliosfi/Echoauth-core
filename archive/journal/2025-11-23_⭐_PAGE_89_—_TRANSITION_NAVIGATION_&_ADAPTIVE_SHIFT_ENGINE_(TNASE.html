EchoAuth recognizes 6 types of shifts:


1️⃣ Activity shift

(play → clean up, TV → homework)


2️⃣ Location shift

(room → hallway, home → school)


3️⃣ Sensory shift

(quiet → loud, dark → bright)


4️⃣ Social shift

(being alone → interacting with others)


5️⃣ Emotional shift

(calm → focused, excited → stillness)


6️⃣ Cognitive shift

(one task → another task)


Each shift has a different stress pattern.

TNASE adapts to ALL.
