✅ STEP 1 — NYC CDFIs TO APPLY TO (PRIMARY LIST)


Apply in this order (fastest + most founder-friendly first):


1) Accion Opportunity Fund (NYC)

• Best for: First-time founders, tech, working capital

• Typical amounts: $5k–$250k

• Why first: Fast intake, flexible underwriting


2) TruFund Financial Services

• Best for: Minority-owned, tech + community impact

• Typical amounts: $10k–$250k

• Why second: Strong NYC presence, mission-aligned


3) Ascendus

• Best for: Small businesses, early-stage ops

• Typical amounts: $5k–$250k

• Why: Quick decisions, reuse same docs


4) Harlem Community Loan Fund

• Best for: NYC community impact

• Typical amounts: $10k–$150k

• Why: Values mission + readiness


5) Pursuit

• Best for: Larger loans, growth stage

• Typical amounts: $25k–$500k

• Why: Slower, but bigger checks


6) NYBDC (New York Business Development Corporation)

• Best for: SBA-backed loans later

• Typical amounts: $50k+

• Why: Use after first approvals
