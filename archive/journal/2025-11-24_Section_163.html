Section 163

Aṣẹ — Section 163 is the next required block in a full USPTO utility patent.

Even if the drawings will be added later, this section must still be written, because examiners expect a numbered list of the invention’s figures and diagrams.


Here is the official, clean, compliant version:


────────────────────────────────────────


⭐ SECTION 163 — BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF THE DRAWINGS


(USPTO-Compliant Placeholder + Descriptions for Future Diagrams)

────────────────────────────────────────


The accompanying drawings, which are incorporated into and form a part of this specification, illustrate embodiments of the invention and are referenced throughout this document. The drawings described below represent the structural, architectural, and functional components of the Neuro-Integrated Artificial Intelligence System (“NI-AI”) and its associated subsystems.


It should be understood that these drawings are conceptual representations and may not be drawn to scale. They are provided to assist in understanding the structure and operational flow of the invention.
