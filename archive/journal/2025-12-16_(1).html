NYC SBS — WHAT HAPPENS NEXT (AND HOW TO HANDLE IT)


1️⃣ You’ll likely get one of these

• An email intake confirmation

• A request to schedule a short call (15–30 min)

• Or a referral to a partner org (very common)


None of these are tests. They’re triage.


⸻


2️⃣ HOW TO ANSWER THE FIRST ADVISOR CALL


(This is the part that matters most)


Keep your answers short and grounded


If they ask “What are you building?”


We’re building EchoAuth, a caregiver-assisted support system for non-verbal and neurodivergent individuals. We’re in prototype development and preparing for early pilots.


If they ask “Do you have revenue?”


Not yet. We’re pre-revenue and focused on completing the prototype and pilot readiness.


If they ask “What kind of funding are you looking for?”


We’re looking for early-stage support — pilot funding, stipends, or program-based capital — not traditional loans.


If they ask “Why now?”


We’ve defined the system architecture and are at the point where targeted support would help us move from prototype into pilot validation.


That’s it. No philosophy. No tech deep-dive unless they ask.


⸻


3️⃣ WHAT TO HAVE READY (MENTALLY, NOT DOCUMENTS)


You may be asked:

• ✔ Business name & NYC location

• ✔ What problem you solve (1–2 sentences)

• ✔ What milestone comes next (prototype → pilot)

• ✔ Who benefits (caregivers / families)


You do not need:

• financial projections

• pitch decks

• code

• patents

• revenue proof


Not at this stage.


⸻


4️⃣ WHAT PROGRAMS THEY MAY ROUTE YOU TO


Just so you recognize the language when you hear it:

• “Founder support”

• “Capital access referral”

• “Pilot or innovation program”

• “Disability-aligned services”

• “Workforce or family stability”


All of these are good signals.


⸻


5️⃣ IMPORTANT: WHAT NOT TO DO

• ❌ Don’t say “I was denied by Accion”

(Just say: we’re pre-revenue)

• ❌ Don’t frame this as desperation

• ❌ Don’t ask for large sums upfront

• ❌ Don’t over-justify your legitimacy


You’re asking for guidance + access, not permission.
