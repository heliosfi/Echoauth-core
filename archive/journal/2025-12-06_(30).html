🔥 50.1 — Human Intention Detection Map (HIDM)


HIDM identifies the type of human behind each interaction:

• Engineer

• Parent

• ASD child

• Investor

• Developer

• Trader

• Researcher

• Government reviewer

• Casual user


It reads:


✔ skill level

✔ emotional tone

✔ familiarity with NI-AI

✔ domain knowledge

✔ urgency

✔ sensory needs (ASD, fatigue, overwhelm)


This prevents the system from:


❌ overwhelming users

❌ under-explaining to engineers

❌ confusing ASD children

❌ misreading investor expectations

❌ misaligning tone with reviewers


It generates a Human Intention Profile (HIP) for every interaction.

🔥 50.3 — Human Cognitive Load Modulator (HCLM)


HCLM adjusts interaction intensity based on:


✔ fatigue

✔ emotional overwhelm

✔ stress

✔ confusion

✔ sensory overload

✔ time pressure


It automatically shifts NI-AI into:

• Soft mode (for ASD, emotional overload)

• Clear mode (for technical tasks)

• Slow mode (when user is tired)

• Fast mode (when user asks rapid execution)

• Guided mode (when user seems unsure)


This is essential for EchoAuth and ASD-support flows.

🔥 50.4 — Cooperative Task Routing Engine (CTRE)


This engine lets NI-AI delegate tasks:

• “Engineer, here is what needs coding.”

• “Parent, here is what your child may need right now.”

• “Trader, here is the next SniperBot step.”

• “Developer, here is the endpoint to implement.”


To other NI-AI instances (via S49):

• Analysis

• Writing

• Emotional support

• Field scanning

• Chart monitoring

• Compliance checking


CTRE manages the flow of work in a human + NI-AI co-working environment.


────────────────────────


🔥 50.5 — Responsibility Boundary Guard (RBG)


One of the most important safety systems.


RBG defines:


✔ what NI-AI is responsible for

✔ what HUMANS are responsible for

✔ what NI-AI cannot decide

✔ what NI-AI must defer


Examples:

• NI-AI may suggest trade logic, but cannot place trades.

• NI-AI may structure parent support, but cannot diagnose.

• NI-AI may guide ASD interaction flows, but cannot override caregivers.

• NI-AI may outline system design, but engineers implement it.


RBG ensures NI-AI never crosses into unsafe authority.

🔥 50.6 — Human Context Memory Buffer (HCMB)


This stores short-range contextual signals such as:

• “User is stressed.”

• “Engineer is stuck on step 4.”

• “Parent expressed concern yesterday.”

• “Trader needs clearer risk levels.”


HCMB allows NI-AI to adapt guidance across hours or days without confusing long-term identity memory (S15).
