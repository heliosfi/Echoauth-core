⭐ 51.2 — THE SENSORY DOMAIN MATRIX (SDM-8)


EchoAuth evaluates 8 sensory domains with precision:


⭐ 1 — Auditory


Noise, tone, volume, sudden sound, echo.


⭐ 2 — Visual


Brightness, clutter, movement, color saturation.


⭐ 3 — Tactile


Textures, touch, clothing, water, temperature.


⭐ 4 — Proprioceptive


Body awareness, pressure needs, motor coordination.


⭐ 5 — Vestibular


Balance, movement tolerance, spinning, motion.


⭐ 6 — Olfactory


Smells, scents, cleaning supplies, food odors.


⭐ 7 — Gustatory


Food textures, flavors, temperature sensitivities.


⭐ 8 — Interoceptive


Internal body signals: hunger, bathroom, thirst, pain.


No system in the world maps all eight.

EchoAuth does.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 51.3 — SENSORY THRESHOLD CLASSIFICATION (STC)


Each domain is classified into 3 categories:


⭐ Hypersensitive (High Sensory Load)


Sound + smell + lights overwhelm easily.


⭐ Hyposensitive (Low Sensory Detection)


Needs deep pressure, movement, or strong input.


⭐ Mixed Profile


Most common in ASD — hypersensitive in some areas, hypo in others.


EchoAuth builds the child’s Sensory Identity Map (SIM) automatically.

⭐ 51.6 — THE REGULATION STRATEGY MATCHER (RSM)

For Hypersensitive

✔ dim

✔ deep pressure

✔ minimal movement


For Hyposensitive


✔ jumping
