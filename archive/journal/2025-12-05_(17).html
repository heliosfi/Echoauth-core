Business Purpose

EchoAuth™ is a biometric, voice-anchored, safety and identity system designed specifically for autistic and non-verbal children, their families, and the caregivers who support them.

The system provides safe authentication, emotional-state detection, and protected communication between a child, parent, and caregiver.


CDFI funding will allow EchoAuth to reach operational readiness, complete regulatory-required components, and prepare for partnerships with ASD daycare centers, schools, clinical care providers, and family homes.


Use of Funds

CDFI funds will be applied to essential build components only — no personal expenses — ensuring maximum impact and rapid deployment:

1. Engineering & Software Development – $18,000

• Finalizing the EchoAuth Kernel (HER/Safety Spine v1.4)

• Integration of biometric modules (voice, rhythm, coherence patterns)

• Mobile App (iOS/Android) & caregiver dashboard

• Security: JWT, encryption, compliance (FERPA, COPPA, ADA alignment)

2. Hardware Prototype + Wearable Integration – $7,000

• EchoAuth wearable band (kinetic-energy charging)

• Sensors: movement, safety patterns, tactile input

• Prototyping tools + testing with ASD advocates

• Wearable donation mode (athlete charity model)

3. Licensing, Insurance, and Compliance – $4,000

• State childcare/medical-adjacent compliance filing

• Professional liability to protect families and institutions

• Required software licensing, authentication libraries

4. Pilots with ASD Institutions – $6,000

• Piloting with daycare centers, schools, and clinical care programs

• Training onboarding for staff (one-time caregiver fee model)

• Testing safe communication chain between child, caregiver, and parent

5. Initial Marketing + Outreach – $2,500

• Community demonstrations

• Awareness partnerships (ASD networks, local organizations)

• Materials for pilot institutions


Total Requested: $37,500


EchoAuth enters revenue mode as soon as pilots begin, ensuring CDFI repayment feasibility.


Revenue Model (Refined)

EchoAuth was redesigned to remove financial strain from families:

• Parents (Home Use):

Affordable subscription options for families who can pay

• hardship program for low-income families

• Institutions (Daycare, Schools, Clinical Care):

Subscription-based (monthly/annual)

→ deductible as an education or clinical safety expense

• Independent Caregivers (Nannies, Aides, Non-Institutional):

One-time onboarding fee only

→ gives them training + access to parent dashboard link

→ no recurring financial burden

• Wearable Charity Energy Model:

Athletes & influencers can wear EchoAuth-enabled gear that converts kinetic energy → sponsorship credits, supporting free or reduced-cost EchoAuth plans for families in financial hardship.


This structure ensures nobody is left behind and aligns perfectly with CDFI values: accessibility, community uplift, and long-term stability.


Repayment Plan

Repayment is feasible because revenue begins early:

• Institutional subscriptions start as soon as pilot partners convert.

• Parent subscriptions begin during the public launch in 2025.

• Wearable-donation fund reduces operational pressure while supporting inclusivity.


Monthly repayment will be covered by subscription revenue and early licensing agreements with ASD programs.
