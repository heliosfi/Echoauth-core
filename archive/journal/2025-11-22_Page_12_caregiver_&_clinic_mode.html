PAGE 12 — CAREGIVER & CLINIC MODE


Insight Engine • Safe Reporting • Ethical Data Layer


────────────────────────


EchoAuth is a child-first system.

Caregiver Mode exists ONLY to support the child — never to judge them, diagnose them, or track them in a clinical way.


This is the safest and most ethical autism-support design on the market.


────────────────────────


⭐ 12.1 — CAREVIEW ENGINE (CVE)


The bridge between child sessions and caregiver insights.


The CareView Engine converts:

• emotional data

• routine success

• overload events

• interaction patterns

• pacing preferences

• sensory indicators


into gentle, non-clinical summaries.


NEVER includes:

• psychiatric labels

• judgments

• negative language

• deficit framing

• diagnostic speculation


ALWAYS includes:

• supportive insights

• calming suggestions

• routine improvements

• emotional patterns

• successful strategies


────────────────────────


⭐ 12.2 — DAILY SESSION DIGEST


Soft, safe, non-clinical recap for caregivers.


Example output:


“Today they preferred short steps.

A brief calming routine helped.

They responded well to slow pacing in the afternoon.”


Content must always follow:

• neutral tone

• no blame

• no urgency unless safety risk detected

• simple 2–4 sentence summary


────────────────────────


⭐ 12.3 — EMOTIONAL TREND MAP (ETM-C)


Caregiver-friendly version of Page 11’s emotional mapping.


Caregivers see:

• average mood trend

• overload frequency

• routine success score

• preferred pacing

• stable times of day

• known triggers

• calming strategies that worked


Visualized with:

• simple bar charts

• soft color palette

• no red (avoid danger signaling)

• no alerts unless critical


────────────────────────


⭐ 12.4 — ROUTINE PERFORMANCE INSIGHTS


Tracks how well each routine supports the child.


Metrics include:

• completion rate

• steps skipped

• time taken

• number of safe-mode shifts

• post-routine emotional change


Caregiver examples:


“Morning Routine worked well today.”

“Homework Routine improved after a short break.”

“Bedtime Routine may need fewer steps tonight.”


EchoAuth frames everything as guidance, not evaluation.


────────────────────────


⭐ 12.5 — CALMING STRATEGY RECOMMENDER


EchoAuth intelligently suggests the best next routine.


Triggered by patterns such as:

• “Evening fatigue rising”

• “Homework stress detected”

• “Sensory overload repeating”


Possible suggestions:


“Try a 3-step calming routine next time.”

“A shorter morning routine may help.”

“Consider starting homework with a Task Starter Routine.”


This is not medical advice — it is pattern-based support.


────────────────────────


⭐ 12.6 — SAFETY & OVERLOAD MONITORING


Gently informs caregivers when Safe Mode was used.


Caregiver output:


“EchoAuth activated Safe Mode twice today to support emotional regulation.”


Important:

• NO emotional labeling

• NO behavioral judgment

• NO alarms unless physical risk detected


Only safe, factual patterns.


────────────────────────


⭐ 12.7 — CLINIC MODE (OPTIONAL FEATURE)


For therapists, behavioral specialists, and clinics.


EchoAuth Clinic Mode includes:

• anonymized trend summaries

• behavior arcs

• sensory patterns

• attention cycles

• emotional recovery speed

• routine effectiveness over months


Clinicians receive:

• long-term graphs

• weekly summaries

• intervention suggestions

• structured prompts for sessions


All anonymized.

All ethically safe.

No sensitive conversation logs.


────────────────────────


⭐ 12.8 — ETHICAL DATA FILTER (EDF)


Protects the child from emotional over-exposure.


EchoAuth NEVER shares:

• emotional confessions

• family conflicts

• self-critical statements

• embarrassing moments

• personal stories

• any trauma content


This data is not stored, not forwarded, and not visible to caregivers.


CareView shows patterns, not private feelings.
