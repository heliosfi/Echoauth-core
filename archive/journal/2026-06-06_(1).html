MARLI: THE SKELETON KING


Vision


A Roblox action-adventure game where players battle monsters, gather resources, rebuild cities, unlock powerful abilities, and ultimately defeat the Skeleton King.


The game teaches persistence, strategy, building, leadership, and progression through gameplay rather than lectures.
