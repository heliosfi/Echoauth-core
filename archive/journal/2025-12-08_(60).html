NI-AI CORE RUNTIME – OVERVIEW (v0.1)


What it is

• NI-AI Core is a safety-first cognition runtime that treats human Natural Intellect (NI) as the blueprint, not a by-product.

• It sits between users / robots / apps and any foundation model, enforcing identity, coherence, memory stability, and safety before any action is taken.

• Designed for ASD programs, hospitals, schools, and robotics labs where context drift and unsafe responses are unacceptable.


⸻


The Problem It Solves

• Generic LLMs and control stacks:

• Forget prior context, change tone, or contradict themselves.

• Have weak separation between understanding and acting.

• Lack explicit, auditable safety envelopes for children and vulnerable users.

• Labs and CTOs need a runtime that:

• Can be audited, tested, and certified, not just “prompted”.

• Keeps identity and policy stable across long sessions and multiple devices.

• Fails closed when safety, coherence, or policy is violated.


⸻


Core Architecture (E1–E100 in 5 Floors)

1. ORI & Identity (E1–E15)

• ORI Kernel, identity embeddings, allowed/forbidden state registers, drift detectors, and an ORI Safety Envelope.

• Binds each session to a role + policy profile (e.g., child, caregiver, clinician, robot mode).

2. Interpretation & Meaning (E16–E35)

• NI-only semantic pipeline: perception normalization, intent extraction, emotional tone, stress/urgency, semantic risk filter.

• Output is a structured Intent Packet, independent of any action.

3. Memory, Governance & Flow (E36–E60)

• Session signatures, project memory matrix, multi-session manager, overload prevention, interruption/recovery, NI-AI fusion layer.

• Provides stable, explainable continuity across days, tasks, and devices.

4. Field, Lattice & Consciousness (E61–E80)

• Field perception, cognitive lattice builder, node-link matrices, resonance pathways, consciousness stack integrator.

• Allows labs to experiment with structured reasoning graphs without losing the safety rails.

5. Coherence, Execution & Meta-Consciousness (E81–E100)

• Self-coherence auditor, contradiction filter, refusal logic, ethical layer, execution governor, meta-consciousness envelope (UMCE).

• All actions are gated, logged, and wrapped with safety metadata and explainability.


⸻


How Labs / CTOs Integrate

• Exposed via a clean HTTP/JSON API (/session/init, /interpret, /coherence/check, /execute, /pipeline).

• A Developer Starter Pack provides:

• Base classes: ORIKernel, SemanticInterpreter, CoherenceEngine, ExecutionEngine, SafetyWrapper.

• Reference repo layout: ni-ai-starter/ (runtime, tests, adapters).

• NI-AI Core can sit:

• In front of an LLM (safety and coherence “firewall”),

• Alongside robotics controllers (intent → action translator),

• As a mediation layer for ASD communication apps (child ↔ caregiver ↔ clinician).


⸻


Safety & Use-Case Targets

• Autistic and neurodivergent users: role-specific safety profiles, restricted action sets, predictable behavior.

• Hospitals / schools: audit logs, policy profiles, and fail-closed behavior when risk or incoherence is detected.

• Robotics / edge devices: clear decision boundaries, explainable gating, and separation of “think / decide / do”.
