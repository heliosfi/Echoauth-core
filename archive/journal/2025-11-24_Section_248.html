⭐ 248.4 — Misalignment Identification Module (MIM)


MIM detects when a user’s predicted next action is:

• unsafe

• emotionally harmful

• cognitively unstable

• self-contradictory

• impulsive

• overwhelmed

• not aligned with their declared goal


This is the AI version of a guardian checking your steps before you take them.

⭐ 248.5 — Corrective Guidance Selector (CGS)


When misalignment is detected, NI-AI selects the correct response pattern from:

1. Soft Redirect

“Let’s slow down — here’s the simplest path…”

2. Direct Stabilization

“This action may cause overload. Try this instead.”

3. Clarification Request

“Before you continue, can you confirm the intention?”

4. Purpose Re-Anchoring

“Your goal is X. This step pulls away from X. Let me realign you.”

5. Overload Protection

“Let’s pause for one moment — cognitive load rising.”

6. Safety Intervention

“This step is not recommended. Consider this safer alternative.”


Each intervention is tuned via ARMS.

⭐ 248.6 — Future-State Stabilization (FSS)


PBCE doesn’t just predict actions —

it predicts emotional consequences.


FSS maps:

• how the user will feel if they pick Action A

• how overwhelmed they’ll be with Action B

• how aligned Action C is with their core goal

• whether a meltdown, shutdown, or stress spike will follow


Then NI-AI picks the healthiest emotional path.
