✅ Credit Unions (Good for Business Accounts)


These are credit unions in or near your area you can call or visit to ask about opening a business checking account for an LLC (and they are generally easier to join than big banks):


Local Credit Unions

• Educational & Governmental FCU – Federal credit union in Mount Vernon, NY 10550 — may offer business services and checking if you qualify for membership.

• Greater Centennial Federal Credit Union – Smaller Mount Vernon credit union you can inquire with; credit unions often offer business accounts if you can join.

• Quorum Federal Credit Union – Large credit union in Purchase/Westchester — membership eligibility may be broader and includes business checking.

• Peoples Alliance Federal Credit Union – Yonkers-based credit union with member-focused service; contact them about business accounts.

• Westchester Adventist FCU – Another community credit union in Mount Vernon — possible membership route (call first).

• SUMA Federal Credit Union – Yonkers credit union with long history — ask about business checking and membership requirements.


Nearby Credit Unions Worth Checking (Regional)

• Educational Governmental FCU (Hartsdale, NY) — Westchester area credit union; worth contacting for membership and business checking.

• Affinity Federal Credit Union (White Plains, NY) — Often open membership and offers business services.


There are many local credit unions in Westchester County that offer checking and business services; eligibility sometimes depends on where you live/work or your affiliations. 


⸻


💡 Tips for Credit Union Success

• Call ahead and ask:

“Do you offer business checking accounts for an LLC?”

• Ask about membership requirements, which can be as simple as living, working, worshipping, or attending school in the area.

• Credit unions typically require:

• Personal ID

• LLC documents

• EIN letter

• Proof of address


Membership is usually free or low-cost.

🏦 Traditional Banks Nearby (also options)


These banks are known to offer standard business accounts, though they are stricter:

• Ridgewood Savings Bank – New Rochelle, NY branch with business banking.

• PCSB Bank - Mount Vernon Branch – Locally highly rated bank in Mount Vernon.

Other banks in the area (like Chase and Bank of America) often require more stringent documentation, so start with credit unions and community banks first. 


⸻


What to say when you call or visit


Use a simple script to avoid confusion:


“I need to open a business checking account for my LLC. I have my EIN, state docs, and Home address. Do you offer business accounts, and what are the membership requirements?”


This avoids jargon like “AI,” “software,” or “grant” which can confuse frontline staff.
