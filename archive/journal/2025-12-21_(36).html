Step 2 — Solution Overview (EchoAuth, One Paragraph)


This is where your system shines


EchoAuth is a child-first care coordination and access platform that enables non-verbal children to safely access sensory, music, and engagement programs while allowing parents, caregivers, schools, and clinicians to document development, coordinate care, and track routines. The system preserves parental authority, supports professional contribution, and provides auditable, non-interpretive logging suitable for institutional use.
