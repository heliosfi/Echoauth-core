SniperBot — Precise Definition (Canonical)


SniperBot is a rule-governed trade execution and risk management system.

It does not predict markets. It enforces discipline.


Core Purpose


Execute trades only when predefined structural, risk, and context conditions are met, and manage exits automatically to preserve capital.


⸻


What SniperBot IS

• A bounded execution engine

• A risk-first system

• A human-authorized trading assistant

• A discipline enforcer


⸻


What SniperBot is NOT

• ❌ Not a predictive AI

• ❌ Not autonomous

• ❌ Not continuous trading

• ❌ Not pattern-hunting without context


⸻


Functional Breakdown


1. Default State

• NO TRADE

• The system is idle unless conditions are explicitly satisfied.


⸻


2. Pre-Trade Gate (Must Pass All)


SniperBot arms only if:

• Market structure is identified (trend, range, breakout)

• Micro market news is cleared (no high-risk events unresolved)

• Volatility regime is classified (≈3.5% day vs ≥6% day)

• Liquidity and spread are acceptable


Failure of any gate = no execution


⸻


3. Entry Logic


Entries are structure-based, not indicator-based:

• Break & retest

• Reclaim of key level

• VWAP flip + hold

• Higher-low / lower-high confirmation


Indicators are confirmation only, never the trigger.


⸻


4. Risk Management (Primary Authority)

• Risk per trade defined as a fixed % of account

• Position size auto-calculated based on:

• Stop distance

• Volatility regime

• Capital limits


If required stop is too wide → trade is rejected


⸻


5. Stop Loss

• Placed where the trade thesis is invalid

• Optional additions:

• Time-based stop

• Daily max-loss lockout


⸻


6. Take Profit Logic

• Structured exit ladder:

• Partial at first logical target

• Secondary target at extension

• Optional runner with trailing stop


No discretionary chasing.


⸻


7. Adaptive Behavior

• On ~3.5% range days:

• Tighter targets

• Faster exits

• Reduced frequency

• On ≥6% expansion days:

• Wider structure-valid stops

• Larger targets

• Scaling logic


⸻


8. Governance

• Human retains final authority

• System halts on ambiguity

• No trade is a valid outcome

• Overtrading prevention enforced


⸻


One-Line Identity (Lock This)


SniperBot is disciplined execution under governance, not intelligence under autonomy.


That’s it.

No excess. No mystique. No drift.
