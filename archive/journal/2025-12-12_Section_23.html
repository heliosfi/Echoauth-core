👉 Caregiver-Facing Onboarding Outline (Clean, Trust-Building)


This is what caregivers actually see.

It aligns with the one-time payment, avoids “training vibes,” and reinforces that the system—not the caregiver—carries the burden.


⸻


CAREGIVER ONBOARDING


One-Time Orientation | Calm by Design


What this is


A short, guided orientation to understand how the system protects your child and what you are (and are not) responsible for.


What this is not

• Not therapy

• Not behavior training

• Not ongoing classes

• Not judgment or evaluation


You are not being trained to do more.

You are being oriented so you can do less.


⸻


MODULE 1 — WHAT THIS SYSTEM DOES (5–10 min)


Plain language

• The system is designed to wait

• Nothing happens automatically

• Calm and non-action are successful outcomes


What you should feel

• Relief

• No pressure to “get it right”


⸻


MODULE 2 — SPACES & MOVEMENT (10 min)


Plain language

• Different spaces mean different levels of permission

• Moving forward is always optional

• Staying where you are is always allowed


Key reassurance


Your child is never pushed forward by the system.


⸻


MODULE 3 — WEARABLES (5–10 min)


Plain language

• Wearables do not control your child

• They do not give commands

• They do not judge behavior


What wearables do

• Quietly reflect what state the system is in

• Help everyone stay oriented


⸻


MODULE 4 — WHEN THINGS FEEL HARD (10 min)


Plain language

• Stress or overwhelm means we slow down

• Nothing needs to be completed

• Returning to observation is always correct


Important message


Pausing is not a setback. Pausing is the system working.


⸻


MODULE 5 — WHAT YOU NEVER HAVE TO DO (5 min)


You never have to:

• Push your child forward

• Interpret signals

• Make something happen

• Decide if it’s “time”


The system will not ask that of you.


⸻


MODULE 6 — STOPPING & SAFETY (5 min)


Plain language

• You can stop or pause at any time

• You do not need to explain why

• Stopping is protected and logged as appropriate use


⸻


COMPLETION (2–3 min)

• A simple acknowledgment (not a test)

• Confirms you understand the boundaries

• Activates your access


No scores. No pass/fail. No pressure.


⸻


CAREGIVER PROMISE (THIS IS KEY)


You are paying once to understand the system,

so the system can protect your child without relying on you.


This is what justifies the one-time onboarding fee ethically and clearly.


⸻


WHAT THIS UNLOCKS (WITHOUT ADDING RESPONSIBILITY)

• Clear expectations

• Reduced stress

• Fewer decisions

• Stronger trust in the environment
