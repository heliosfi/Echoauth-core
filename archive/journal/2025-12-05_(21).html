3. Loan Purpose (Clear & Direct):

I am requesting a $15,000 Kiva loan to complete the development and deployment of EchoAuth™, a breakthrough biometric and emotional-safety system designed specifically for non-verbal and semi-verbal autistic children.


The loan will allow me to:

• Finalize the EchoAuth software architecture (AI safety spine, caregiver protocols, interface).

• Build the production-ready authentication system and testing environment.

• Complete the wearable integration and safety module.

• Prepare the platform for institutional partnerships (daycares, ASD schools, clinical care).


This loan directly funds the final engineering steps so EchoAuth can begin generating revenue and serving families who urgently need safer communication tools.


⸻


4. The Problem We Solve:


Millions of autistic children struggle to communicate distress, needs, or emergencies.

Caregivers and parents often only discover a problem after it’s too late.


EchoAuth solves this by giving non-verbal and semi-verbal children a safe, real-time way to authenticate themselves and express emotional states through AI-assisted signals.


Parents gain peace of mind.

Caregivers gain clarity.

Children gain a voice.


⸻


5. Who We Serve & Why It Matters


EchoAuth supports three groups, each with a payment structure that avoids burdening low-income families:


A) Parents (Direct Families)

• Standard subscription for families who can afford it.

• Low-income families receive an affordable subsidized plan — no child should be denied safety because of finances.


B) Independent Caregivers (non-institution)

Examples: babysitters, nannies, home aides, respite workers.

• One-time onboarding fee only (no subscription).

• This gives caregivers training and system access but does not create monthly bills.


C) Institutions (daycares, ASD schools, clinical care centers)

• Monthly or annual subscription

• Fully tax-deductible as a business/institution expense.

• Includes admin dashboards and multi-child support.


This structure ensures sustainability without financially harming struggling families.


⸻


6. Additional Support Through Wearable Donations


EchoAuth integrates with optional wearable devices that generate micro-donations using kinetic energy (movement).

Sports athletes, volunteers, and supporters can wear them to fund free or discounted access for low-income ASD families.


This creates a community-powered safety fund that grows as the project scales.
