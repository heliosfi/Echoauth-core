Loan Purpose: EchoAuth Prototype Completion


I am seeking a Kiva loan to complete the engineering and prototype of EchoAuth, a neuro-support authentication system designed to protect autistic children and support caregivers during transitions.


This loan is not for scaling or marketing — it is dedicated entirely to:

• finishing the prototype

• testing the sensors and safety system

• certifying the early hardware

• integrating emotional-state detection

• preparing EchoAuth for pilots with ASD families, schools, and clinical partners


This funding helps me close the gap between invention and real-world deployment for families who need this technology today.


⸻


🔹 Why This Matters


Families with autistic children face daily challenges with:

• unsafe or unclear caregiver handoffs

• emotional overload

• communication breakdowns

• stress-triggered meltdowns

• unpredictable transitions


EchoAuth is designed to solve these problems by combining:

• a small wearable sensor

• an emotional-state engine (HER Kernel)

• a secure caregiver-authentication chamber

• real-time alerts and stress-level monitoring

• safety rules that protect the child at every step


By completing the prototype, I can begin early testing with families and prepare EchoAuth for partnerships with:

• ASD therapy centers

• Schools

• Clinics

• Community childcare programs


This loan accelerates the development so that autistic children are safer, caregivers are supported, and families have peace of mind.
