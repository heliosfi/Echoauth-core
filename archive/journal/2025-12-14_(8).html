Project: EchoAuth – Caregiver-First Authentication & Communication System

1. Loan Purpose (Plain Language)


The requested CDFI loan will be used to complete final prototype readiness, security hardening, and controlled pilot deployment of EchoAuth, a caregiver-first system supporting neurodivergent and non-verbal children.


Funds will not be used for speculation, personal expenses, or unrelated business activities.

All uses are directly tied to product readiness, compliance, and early revenue generation.

The loan enables immediate revenue pathways, not speculative growth.
