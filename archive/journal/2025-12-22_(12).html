UNIVERSAL ECHOAuth TALK TRACK


(Safe for parents, clinicians, educators, lenders, and funders)


Opening (10 seconds)


“EchoAuth exists to solve a coordination problem — not a medical one.

When multiple adults are responsible for a child, inconsistency creates risk. The child ends up adapting to adults instead of adults aligning around the child.”


⸻


What EchoAuth Is (clear + safe)


“EchoAuth is parent-governed coordination infrastructure.

It’s not diagnostic, not therapeutic, and not behavioral analysis.

It enforces parent-approved rules so help can happen safely and consistently.”


⸻


Why It’s Needed (the concern frame)


“In real life, help comes from concerned people — parents, aides, teachers, clinicians, family members.

Concern alone isn’t enough. Without shared boundaries, even good intentions can create confusion or harm.”


“EchoAuth turns concern into correct action by enforcing clear roles.”


⸻


How It Works (plain, non-technical)


“Parents approve routines, supports, and roles once.

Caregivers carry out approved tasks.

Schools and clinicians observe and recommend — but don’t override.


If optional tools like reminders or sensors are used, they provide context only — time, place, routine — never interpretation.

And when something is unclear, the system pauses instead of guessing.”


⸻


Why This Is Better for the Child (human-centered)


“The child experiences consistency instead of constant adjustment.

Fewer conflicting expectations.

Less stress from handoffs.

More calm, predictable support.”


⸻


What EchoAuth Does Not Do (important for reviewers)


“EchoAuth does not diagnose.

It does not prescribe.

It does not automate decisions.

It does not replace parents, teachers, or clinicians.”


“It keeps authority where it belongs — with parents — while allowing others to help safely.”


⸻


Why This Matters to This Room (works for everyone)


“For families, it reduces burnout.

For educators, it improves continuity.

For clinicians, it prevents misalignment.

For institutions and funders, it lowers risk by enforcing governance instead of guesswork.”


⸻


Close (one sentence, memorize this)


“EchoAuth enforces parent-approved coordination so the child doesn’t have to adapt to every adult.”
