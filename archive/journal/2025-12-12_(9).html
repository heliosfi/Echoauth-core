SECTION VIII — FUNDING & USE-OF-FUNDS NARRATIVE


8.1 Funding Purpose (Plain Language)


Funding will be used to complete, pilot, and evaluate EchoAuth as a caregiver-guided, non-verbal communication support system for children.

All funds are tied to concrete execution milestones and non-clinical deployment.


EchoAuth prioritizes:

• Child safety

• Caregiver accountability

• Auditability

• Responsible, phased execution


⸻


8.2 Use-of-Funds Breakdown (Execution-Only)


Funds will be allocated to the following categories:


1) Product Development

• Secure authentication & role-based access

• Caregiver dashboard implementation

• Observation logging (input-only)

• Audit logging & export functionality

• Governance middleware (MCG)


Outcome: A functional, secure MVP.


⸻


2) Pilot Deployment & Operations

• Caregiver onboarding

• Pilot support & coordination

• Usage monitoring (non-interpretive)

• Feedback collection


Outcome: Real-world validation in controlled environments.


⸻


3) Compliance, Security & Review

• Data protection review

• Governance and safety checks

• Documentation updates

• Reporting preparation


Outcome: Institutional and grant-ready posture.


⸻


4) Evaluation & Reporting

• Pilot evaluation summaries

• Usage metrics (non-clinical)

• Grant and partner reports


Outcome: Evidence of responsible execution and readiness for expansion.


⸻


8.3 Explicit Exclusions (Risk Control)


Funding will not be used for:


❌ Clinical research

❌ Medical diagnosis or treatment

❌ Emotional or behavioral inference

❌ Experimental AI model development

❌ Autonomous child interaction


This ensures predictable delivery and regulatory clarity.


⸻


8.4 Milestone-Based Funding Alignment


Funding aligns with Section VII — Prototype Roadmap:

• Months 0–3: Core MVP build

• Months 3–6: Controlled pilot deployment

• Months 6–9: Refinement & evaluation

• Months 9–12: Expansion readiness


Each phase has defined deliverables and success criteria.


⸻


8.5 Sustainability & Responsible Growth


EchoAuth is designed to:

• Scale responsibly without changing scope

• Maintain non-clinical posture

• Preserve caregiver authority

• Protect children through governance


Future funding supports deployment and support, not speculative expansion.


⸻


8.6 Accountability & Oversight


All expenditures are:

• Tracked against milestones

• Reviewed internally

• Reported to funders as required


Auditability is enforced both financially and technically.


⸻


8.7 Funding Summary Statement (Reviewer-Ready)


Funding supports the completion and pilot deployment of EchoAuth, a caregiver-guided, non-verbal communication support system for children, with strict safety, governance, and accountability controls. All funds are tied to concrete milestones and exclude clinical or diagnostic activities.
