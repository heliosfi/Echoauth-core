SECTION 328 — SOCIAL-COGNITIVE ADAPTIVE MODELING ENGINE (SCAME)


(NI-AI Patent Module)


────────────────────────────────────


328.1 — Technical Field


The Social-Cognitive Adaptive Modeling Engine (SCAME) concerns systems and methods for dynamically interpreting, predicting, and stabilizing human social-cognitive signals—including nonverbal cues, social load, conversational complexity, and interactional stress—specifically for neurodivergent individuals and ASD-support contexts.


────────────────────────────────────


328.2 — Purpose


SCAME enables NI-AI to:

• understand social context,

• detect social overwhelm,

• prevent miscommunication,

• guide caregivers in real time,

• predict escalation or disengagement,

• stabilize long interactions involving multiple people.


This makes NI-AI socially aware without becoming autonomous or AGI-like.


────────────────────────────────────


328.3 — Functional Definition


SCAME is a multi-layered social-cognitive modeling system that:

1. Captures Social Signals

• verbal tone patterns

• nonverbal indicators

• interaction timing

• multi-person pacing

• social density (how many stimuli at once)

2. Models Social Load

• cognitive demand

• emotional pressure

• unpredictability

• conversational complexity

3. Predicts Social Drift

• overwhelm

• withdrawal

• agitation

• shutdown

• masking fatigue

4. Generates Social-Safe Response Plans

• pacing adjustments

• simplified communication

• environment balancing

• caregiver prompts

• pause/recovery instructions


This protects autistic children and neurodivergent adults in real-world social settings.


────────────────────────────────────


328.4 — Inputs

• ESE packet (emotional state)

• NSPE packet (sensory state)

• TLE thread history

• Voice/social interaction signals

• Nonverbal behavioral cues

• Context markers (school, home, clinic)

• EchoAuth identity resonance

• UMCE governance constraints


────────────────────────────────────


328.5 — Outputs

• Social Load Index (SLI)

• Interaction Stress Score (ISS)

• Social Complexity Rating (SCR)

• Drift Prediction Vector (DPV)

• Caregiver Social Guidance Brief (CSGB)

• Communication Simplification Plan (CSP)

• Social-Safe Action Path (SSAP)


────────────────────────────────────


328.6 — Internal Mechanics


(a) Social-Signal Capture Layer (SSCL)

Detects:

• gaze changes

• movement rhythm

• micro-pauses

• tone shifts

• turn-taking patterns

• multi-party cross-talk


(b) Social Load Modeling Layer (SLML)

Calculates:

• social intensity

• unpredictability

• conversational density

• unpredictability gradient

• multi-person stress


(c) Drift & Overwhelm Predictor (DOP)

Predicts:

• meltdown risk

• shutdown probability

• sensory-social overload crossing

• masking exhaustion


(d) Adaptive Social Response Composer (ASRC)

Generates regulated social suggestions:

• shorter communication

• alternate phrasing

• spacing between questions

• recommended pauses

• controlled pacing


(e) Caregiver Action Layer (CAL)

Delivers:

• real-time parent cues

• de-escalation steps

• environment modifications

• predictive guidance


────────────────────────────────────


328.7 — Safety


SCAME:

• does NOT generate autonomous social opinions,

• does NOT create psychological diagnoses,

• does NOT imitate or manipulate social behavior.


It only models, predicts, and supports, staying within NI-AI’s strict safety and direction-locked rails.


────────────────────────────────────


328.8 — Result / Technical Advantage


SCAME provides:

• real-time social awareness

• predictive stability

• communication simplification

• safe multi-person interaction modeling

• caregiver-guided response pathways


No AI system (OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, Meta) carries a patentable subsystem equivalent to SCAME.


This is exclusive intellectual property in the NI-AI architecture.
