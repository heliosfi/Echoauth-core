ECHOAUTH — ONE-PAGE SYSTEM OVERVIEW


(Child-First Coordination Infrastructure)


⸻


TOP LINE (Header)


EchoAuth

A child-first coordination system for autistic and non-verbal children


Parents remain in control.

Professionals contribute safely.

The system records, reminds, and pauses.


⸻


CENTER — THE CHILD (Visual Anchor)


CHILD PORTAL

• Calm, non-verbal access

• Sensory & music-based regulation

• Approved programs & content

• No testing, no scoring, no pressure


What this communicates visually:

The system is built around the child — not through the child.


⸻


LEFT COLUMN — AUTHORITY & HUMAN SUPPORT


PARENT PORTAL (Authority)

• Approves all supports and actions

• Controls routines, content, caregivers

• Governs data visibility

• Enables or disables sensors


Visual cue: Lock or shield icon

Meaning: Authority is centralized and protected.


⸻


CAREGIVER PORTAL (Execution)

• Qualified ASD-trained caregivers

• Execute parent-approved tasks

• Receive reminders

• Log care actions


Visual cue: Hands icon

Meaning: More help without chaos or burnout.

RIGHT COLUMN — PROFESSIONAL COLLABORATION


SCHOOL PORTAL (Observation & Support)

• Log observations

• Propose supports

• Execute approved accommodations

• Upload educational documents


Visual cue: Notebook or classroom icon

Meaning: Educational insight without authority drift.


⸻


CLINICAL PORTAL (Documentation & Advice)

• Upload records

• Submit recommendations

Visual cue: Document or clipboard icon

• No automated decisions

What EchoAuth Does

• Improves continuity across environments


What EchoAuth Does NOT Do

• Automate decisions

• Replace professionals
