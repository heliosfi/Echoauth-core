56.1 Purpose of the Thread-Linking Engine

56.2 The Five Parallel Cognitive Threads


The TLE maintains 5 always-on threads:


1️⃣ Emotional Thread (E-Thread)

Tracks the child’s emotional state:

• calm, curious, anxious, fearful, frustrated, shut down, overloaded

• pulls from: ORI emotional layer, NI ERI, NSM state, KFI_EmotionalContour


2️⃣ Cognitive Thread (C-Thread)

Tracks cognitive load & processing:

• confusion, understanding, focus, slow processing, mental fatigue

• pulls from: PNPE cognitive rhythm, NSM, ORI patterns


3️⃣ Contextual Thread (X-Thread)

Tracks the situation:

• where we are, what time it is, what’s happening, what just happened

• pulls from: environment mode (home/school/clinic/public), LDMS timeline, NI context


4️⃣ Device/Environment Thread (D-Thread)

Tracks active devices & environment:

• which devices are online (bracelet, toy, hub)

• what the environment is doing (noise, light, heat, people)

• pulls from: ENARS, environment sensors, school/home hubs


5️⃣ Purpose/Intent Thread (P-Thread)

Tracks why the system is acting:

• are we trying to calm?

• are we trying to prepare for transition?

• are we in a medical context?

• are we in learning mode?

• pulls from: caregiver goals, system state (regulation / monitoring / intervention), NI flags


All 5 threads run together and are never allowed to fall out of sync.
