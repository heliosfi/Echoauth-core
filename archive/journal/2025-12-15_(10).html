1️⃣ KIVA BORROWER PROFILE (PUBLIC-FACING)


Business Overview


Helios F.I LLC is a New York City–based technology company building EchoAuth, a safety-first digital system designed to support caregivers and families working with non-verbal and neurodivergent individuals.


The focus of the business is protective infrastructure, not surveillance or diagnosis. EchoAuth is designed to reduce pressure on families and caregivers by supporting calm, predictable interaction environments where silence and non-participation are respected.


The company is currently in its early deployment phase, preparing pilot-ready software and operational infrastructure.


⸻


The Founder’s Story


I’m a NYC-based founder, parent, and systems builder. My work comes from lived experience supporting neurodivergent care environments and seeing firsthand how many systems unintentionally create pressure instead of safety.


I started Helios F.I LLC to build technology that prioritizes restraint, clarity, and human oversight, rather than automation or control. EchoAuth is the first product in that mission.


This loan helps bridge the gap between concept and deployment — allowing me to move from preparation to active rollout.


⸻


What the Loan Will Be Used For


Kiva funds will be used to support:

• Final software development and testing

• Secure hosting and infrastructure costs

• Compliance preparation for schools, clinics, and caregiver organizations

• Equipment and operating expenses

• Business stability during pilot onboarding


This loan allows the business to continue development without relying on high-interest credit or equity dilution.


⸻


Community Impact


EchoAuth is designed to support:

• Families caring for non-verbal children

• Caregivers and educators working in high-stress environments

• Community organizations that need safer, calmer digital tools


The goal is not scale-at-all-costs, but sustainable, ethical deployment that respects human dignity.


⸻


Loan Repayment Plan


Loan repayment will come from:

• Consulting and integration work

• Pilot program contracts

• Early institutional subscriptions

• Ongoing service revenue


I maintain disciplined expense control and conservative revenue projections to ensure consistent repayment.
