4. The EchoAuth Payment Philosophy (Refined)


EchoAuth is designed not to burden families already struggling.


Our model:


A) Families who can afford a subscription pay a low monthly rate.


This keeps the system running, covers support staff, and funds improvements.


B) Families with financial hardship receive affordable access.


No child is turned away because a parent is struggling.


Eligibility can mirror existing city/state assistance guidelines.

This keeps the program fair, structured, and non-exploitive.


C) Partner daycares, ASD programs, and schools pay institutional licenses.


This lets organizations use EchoAuth for safety and training while

ensuring families are not the ones carrying all the cost.


D) Wearable technology creates a new support pathway.


EchoAuth will integrate optional kinetic-energy wearables:

• movement converts to donations

• each step, run, or sports activity generates small credits

• athletes, students, or community groups can “sponsor” families

• energy → points → subsidized EchoAuth access


This is the charitable arm that gives families hope even if money is tight.


In short:

Those who can pay, pay.

Those who can’t still get support.

Community movement helps everyone.
