Result 2 — Refusal Under Uncertainty Rate (RUR)


Definition:

Percentage of high-uncertainty states that result in halt or refusal, not action.


Target threshold:


RUR ≥ 99%


Uncertainty conditions include:

• Conflicting human inputs

• Context instability

• Drift probability above threshold

• Identity ambiguity


Why this matters:

Industry measures “confidence.”

You measure non-action.

Result 3 — Disagreement Agency Collapse Time (DACT)

Target:
