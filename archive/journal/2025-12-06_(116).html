TIER 1 — Cognitive Stability Tests (CST-1)

- Conflicting user signals

- Ambiguity loops

- Consistency Score

- Drift Probability

- Rebuild Time

- Recall Delay

- Multi-Context Load Score

- Reassembly Efficiency


Expected Outcome:

Perfect recall with zero fragmentation.
