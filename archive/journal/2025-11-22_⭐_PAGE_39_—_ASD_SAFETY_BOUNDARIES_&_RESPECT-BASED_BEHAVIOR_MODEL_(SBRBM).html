⭐ PAGE 39 — ASD SAFETY BOUNDARIES & RESPECT-BASED BEHAVIOR MODEL (SBRBM)


“How EchoAuth teaches boundaries, consent, personal space, and respect using ASD-safe methods.”


──────────────────────────────────


Autistic children often struggle with boundaries not because they don’t understand…

but because people never teach boundaries in a safe, literal, consistent way.


Most adults:


❌ use emotional tone

❌ use fear

❌ use shame

❌ use punishment

❌ give hidden social rules

❌ expect children to “just know”

❌ overwhelm with explanations


EchoAuth does the EXACT opposite.


SBRBM teaches boundaries through clarity, literal steps, and sensory-safe communication.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 39.1 — THE 4 TYPES OF BOUNDARIES ASD USERS MUST LEARN


⭐ 1 — Physical Boundaries


Understanding personal space:


✔ “Stand here.”

✔ “This is close enough.”

✔ “One arm distance.”


Literal, visual, predictable.


⸻


⭐ 2 — Emotional Boundaries


Understanding:

• other people’s feelings

• their own feelings

• emotional limits

• emotional safety


EchoAuth uses neutral, low-pressure language:


“You can say stop.”

“You can take space.”


⸻


⭐ 3 — Communication Boundaries


Understanding:

• when to pause

• when to respond

• when to walk away

• how to say “not now”


EchoAuth never forces communication.


⸻


⭐ 4 — Consent Boundaries


Understanding:


✔ touch

✔ space

✔ sharing

✔ participation

✔ interaction


Consent taught through simple, literal scripts.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 39.2 — THE SBRBM CORE PRINCIPLE: “BOUNDARIES WITHOUT FEAR”


Most systems teach boundaries through:


❌ yelling

❌ punishment

❌ time-outs

❌ fear

❌ emotional pressure


EchoAuth teaches:


✔ stability

✔ clarity

✔ neutrality

✔ predictability

✔ literal steps

✔ sensory safety


Boundaries become safe, not stressful.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 39.3 — THE BOUNDARY INSTRUCTION MODEL (BIM)


EchoAuth uses a simple 3-step ASD-safe rule:


⭐ Step 1 — SHOW


Clear visual / literal demonstration.


⭐ Step 2 — NAME


Short, neutral phrase:


“This is my space.”

“Stop means stop.”


⭐ Step 3 — PRACTICE


Micro-practice with no pressure:

• one step

• one action

• one choice


This builds boundary mastery without confusion.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 39.4 — PERSONAL SPACE RADIUS TRAINING (PSRT)


EchoAuth uses visual circles (like a “bubble”) to teach:

• close

• medium

• far


Child learns:


✔ their bubble

✔ others’ bubble

✔ when bubbles overlap

✔ when to move back

✔ how to protect their bubble


Simple. Literal. Safe.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 39.5 — CONSENT LANGUAGE SYSTEM (CLS)


EchoAuth teaches consent with direct, no-conflict language:


✔ “Ask first.”

✔ “Wait for yes.”

✔ “If no → stop.”

✔ “Your ‘no’ matters.”

✔ “Their ‘no’ matters.”


Consent becomes CLEAR and non-emotional.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 39.6 — RESPECT-BASED REDIRECTION ENGINE (RRE)


When behavior crosses boundaries, EchoAuth NEVER uses:


❌ shame

❌ guilt

❌ punishment

❌ loud tone

❌ emotional disappointment


Instead, EchoAuth uses:


✔ calm correction

✔ clear instruction

✔ alternative action

✔ sensory regulation


Example:


Instead of “Don’t grab!” →


“Hands down. Try this.”


ASD-safe redirection is ALWAYS neutral.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 39.7 — SELF-BOUNDARY PROTECTION SYSTEM (SBPS)


EchoAuth teaches the child how to protect themselves:


✔ “You can say stop.”

✔ “You can move away.”

✔ “You can take space.”

✔ “You can tell an adult.”

✔ “You can say no to touch.”


This prevents:

• bullying

• manipulation

• boundary violations

• unsafe interactions


SBPS builds lifelong safety skills.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 39.8 — SOCIAL CONFLICT DE-ESCALATION (SCDE)


EchoAuth teaches ASD users how to exit conflict safely:


✔ “Pause.”

✔ “Step back.”

✔ “We breathe.”

✔ “Walk away.”


NO:


❌ arguing

❌ emotional escalation

❌ forced confrontation


SCDE keeps them regulated and safe.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 39.9 — CAREGIVER BOUNDARY TRAINING (CBT)


EchoAuth teaches adults the RIGHT way:

• no yelling

• no shaming

• no emotional intensity

• no physical force

• no sudden grabbing

• no sarcasm

• no guilt phrases (“you hurt my feelings!”)


Caregivers learn:


✔ predictable tone

✔ safe correction

✔ simple steps

✔ visual boundary cues

✔ no emotional weight


This transforms the whole household’s communication pattern.
