358.1 — Technical Field


The Zero-Wearable Sensory Mode (ZWSM) relates to systems and methods for ASD-focused real-time behavior monitoring and emotional stabilization without the use of wearable devices.


It uses only:

• phone camera

• phone microphone

• ambient sensors

• device motion detection

• NI-AI micro-pattern inference


No wristbands.

No chest straps.

No EEG pods.

No bulky devices.


This is EchoAuth’s breakthrough in accessibility.

358.2 — Purpose


To provide ASD behavioral support and emotional stabilization even when the child or adult cannot tolerate:

• wearable devices

• clothing-based sensors

• skin-contact tech

• traditional medical devices
