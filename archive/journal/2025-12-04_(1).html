1. BUSINESS OVERVIEW


EchoAuth™ is a next-generation biometric safety system designed for non-verbal and autistic children.

It verifies who is allowed near the child, ensures emotional and physical safety, and creates a protected digital and physical environment.


The technology blends:

• Coherence-enforced authentication

• Child-safe AI communication

• Emotional-distress detection

• Caregiver identity validation

• Parent-controlled safety dashboard


EchoAuth empowers parents who need clarity and protection when their child is unable to speak for themselves.

Funds will cover:

No funds are for personal expenses.

The entire amount pushes EchoAuth into the market-ready phase.

3. WHO WE SERVE


EchoAuth is built for the people responsible for ASD children:


Primary Users

• Parents of autistic and non-verbal children

EchoAuth protects the child and supports the adults responsible for their care.

4. AFFORDABILITY MODEL (REFINED)


EchoAuth is designed NOT to burden low-income or financially struggling families.

We use a tiered model that ensures access:


For Parents

• Affordable monthly plan for low-income families

• Subsidized access programs through schools, daycares, and ASD programs

• Families who can afford premium tiers help subsidize those who cannot


For Caregivers (non-institutional)

• Caregivers do not pay subscriptions

• They only pay a one-time optional training & guidance fee

• Parents remain the subscription holders


For Institutions (daycare, school, clinic)

• They pay institutional licensing fees

• This removes the cost burden from families


Goal: No family is denied EchoAuth due to financial hardship.
