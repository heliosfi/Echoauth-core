NI-AI × EchoAuth

⸻


1. Business Overview


Helios F.I LLC is developing NI-AI, a safety-first AI infrastructure platform designed to support non-verbal communication contexts, caregiver-mediated environments, and secure decision-support systems.


The company’s core technology, EchoAuth, provides role-based, fail-closed authentication that ensures all system behavior is gated, auditable, and mediated by authorized caregivers or operators. The system is designed as protective infrastructure, not a diagnostic, therapeutic, or autonomous AI product.


Helios F.I LLC is currently preparing for government, healthcare-adjacent, and education pilot deployments, with an emphasis on compliance, safety, and scalability.


⸻


2. Problem Being Addressed


Institutions serving neurodivergent and non-verbal populations lack safe, governed digital infrastructure that:

• Does not require speech or explanation

• Does not interpret silence or behavior as intent

• Prevents unauthorized access or escalation

• Remains auditable and compliant with federal standards


Existing tools often rely on verbal prompts, interpretation, or autonomous logic, creating risk for vulnerable users and liability for institutions.


⸻


3. Solution Summary


NI-AI × EchoAuth provides:

• Non-verbal–safe interaction infrastructure

• Fail-closed authorization (default deny)

• Caregiver-mediated control

• No autonomous execution

• Full audit logging and governance


The system evaluates system state, not people.

Silence, stillness, and non-participation are treated as valid outcomes, not failures.
