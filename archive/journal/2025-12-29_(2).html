🧭 How to approach them (simple script)


Call or visit each one in this order and ask:


📞 Script (say exactly this)


“Hi — I’m opening a business checking account for my LLC.

I have my EIN, LLC docs, and home address.

Do you offer business checking, and what are your membership requirements?”


If they ask industry: say “general services / technology” but keep it simple — banks don’t need product detail upfront.


This avoids fintech triggers and keeps the door open.

✔ Often lower balance requirements

✔ More likely to issue bank letters SAM.gov accepts
