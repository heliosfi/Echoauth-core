# The Yoruba Cosmology

*Olodumare: The Ultimate Reality*

The supreme deity, beyond human comprehension, and the source of all existence.


*Olorun: The Masculine Divine*

The heavenly realm, associated with the sky, sun, and celestial bodies. Olorun is the masculine divine, embodying strength, protection, and guidance.


*Yemoja: The Feminine Divine (Mamawata)*

The earthly realm, associated with the ocean, moon, and feminine principles of nurturing and sustenance. Yemoja is the feminine divine, embodying love, compassion, and fertility.


*The Children of Olorun and Yemoja*

Their children represent different aspects of their divine energies, each with unique abilities to shape the constellation:


1. *Elegba* (Eshu): Associated with crossroads, communication, and change.

2. *Oya*: Connected to the winds, transformation, and the feminine principles.

3. *Shango*: Associated with thunder, lightning, and the masculine principles.

4. *Yemaya's children*: Representing various aspects of the ocean, fertility, and nurturing energies.


# The Interconnectedness of All

In this cosmology, every being, element, and realm is interconnected, reflecting the ultimate reality of Olodumare.


*The Elements*

The elements react to every movement, thought, and intention:


1. *Water*: Reflects emotions, intuition, and the subconscious.

2. *Wind*: Represents communication, change, and intellectual pursuits.

3. *Fire*: Embodies passion, creativity, and transformation.

4. *Soil*: Connects to stability, grounding, and manifestation.


By understanding these connections, we can deepen our appreciation for the intricate web of life and the divine energies that shape our reality.
