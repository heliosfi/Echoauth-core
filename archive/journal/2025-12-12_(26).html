To the Accion East Loan Review Committee,


I am writing to request consideration for a $35,000 small business loan to support the execution phase of a technology project under HeliosFi LLC, focused on building a governed cognitive support system for non-verbal and neurodivergent individuals in supervised care environments.


The project, titled Melanin Cognitive Governance (MCG), is not a consumer application and not an autonomous AI system. It is a safety-first governance framework designed to reduce misinterpretation, improve caregiver response, and lower operational risk in environments where communication barriers create safety and compliance challenges.


⸻


Project Purpose


MCG provides a structured way to:

• translate non-verbal signals into stable, interpretable system states

• enforce clear safety and permission boundaries

• ensure all outcomes are human-governed, auditable, and explainable


The system does not diagnose, label, or replace human judgment. It exists solely to support safer decision-routing and escalation in supervised settings such as schools, caregiver organizations, and support programs.


⸻


Use of Loan Funds


The requested funds will be used strictly for execution and validation, including:

• secure workspace / lab access

• transportation for partner and engineering coordination

• engineering laptop and secure development setup

• limited contract engineering support to implement a working prototype

• controlled testing and simulation environment


No funds will be used for marketing, speculative research, or consumer-facing applications.


⸻


Repayment Strategy


Loan repayment will be supported through service-based revenue, including:

• pilot deployments with caregiver and support organizations

• paid setup and configuration services

• ongoing support and governance customization contracts


The business model does not depend on rapid scaling or advertising revenue. It is designed around institutional partnerships and responsible deployment, which aligns with predictable and conservative cash flow.


⸻


Execution Readiness


The system architecture, safety boundaries, and governance framework are fully defined and locked. Funding will enable the transition from finalized design to a working, demonstrable prototype, positioning the project for pilot engagements and contracted services.


As founder, I retain direct oversight of system architecture, safety governance, and engineering coordination to ensure disciplined execution and compliance alignment.
