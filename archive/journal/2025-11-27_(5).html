🔥 Problem Proof Bullets (PPB)

• Catastrophic Drift in LLMs: Long conversations routinely lose topic coherence, producing unreliable or contradictory outputs unacceptable for regulated or safety-critical institutions.

• Reconstructive Memory Hallucination: Current AI systems recombine or infer past context at recall time rather than retrieving isolated stored units, creating false narratives and risky audit inconsistencies.

• Lack of Identity Continuity Anchoring: Standard authentication methods validate login credentials but do not persist identity confidence, environmental resonance, or session continuity across interaction timelines.

• Multi-Output Collision Loops: Competing AI reasoning threads sometimes generate multiple conflicting answers simultaneously, increasing operator stress and degrading user trust.

• No Purpose-Dominant Arbitration Layer: Most AI systems treat cognition and tone adjustment as equal or interchangeable priorities, allowing emotional, UI, or topic-weight override and resulting in instability.

• Neurodivergent Communication Gap: Existing assistive communication devices do not integrate cognitive-thread reliability, environmental pacing adaptation, or non-verbal UX abstraction for ASD and non-verbal child interfaces.

• Portal Submission Denial Risk: State healthcare, funding, and institutional adoption workflows regularly incur high denial rates due to invalid classification, blank fields, or broken trace chains.

• Emergency Reliability Requirement: Hospitals, schools, and first-responders demand communication and identity systems that operate under accessibility compliance and zero-trust integrity, not medical diagnosis issuance.

• Audit & Litigation Vulnerability: Current AI platforms lack tamper-resistant timestamp seals, forensic trace links, capsule isolation, and denial-risk scoring gates critical for government audits or discovery requests.

• Operator Meltdown Probability: Systems designed without mode separation, identity anchoring, or purpose arbitration increase psychological load for overwhelmed caregivers and neuro-sensitive ASD users.
