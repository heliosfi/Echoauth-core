⸻


About the Borrower


My name is Nicholas Carty, and I am a founder, caregiver, and systems builder.


I started Helios F.I LLC to solve a problem I experienced personally and witnessed repeatedly in my community: families and caregivers of non-verbal and neurodivergent children often lack safe, reliable tools to understand needs, maintain trust, and prevent escalation — especially in moments of stress or silence.


This business was not born out of theory. It came from lived experience, responsibility, and the need to build something that protects children while supporting caregivers — without forcing communication, interpretation, or pressure.


⸻


About the Business


Helios F.I is developing EchoAuth, a caregiver-centered communication and safety platform designed to support non-verbal children through:

• predictable, calm interaction patterns

• caregiver-controlled authorization

• non-verbal signal awareness

• safety-first defaults (non-action is always allowed)


The system does not diagnose, label, or replace caregivers.

It exists to support, not override, human judgment.


Our work prioritizes:

• safety over speed

• restraint over automation

• trust over prediction


⸻


How the Loan Will Be Used


This Kiva loan will be used to:

• finalize core software components

• complete testing for caregiver workflows

• prepare pilot deployments with small organizations

• cover essential operating expenses during early rollout


The funds will not be used for speculation, personal spending, or high-risk activity.


⸻


Impact


The impact of this work is community-based and human:

• caregivers gain tools that reduce stress and uncertainty

• non-verbal children are respected without being forced to perform

• families experience safer, calmer environments

• early pilots create pathways for broader access


This work is especially important in under-resourced communities where access to specialized tools is limited.
