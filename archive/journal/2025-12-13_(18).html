Pricing & Access Model

EchoAuth uses a tiered access model in which individual caregivers pay a one-time onboarding fee, institutions such as clinics, schools, and care agencies operate under a subscription agreement, and parents may optionally subscribe based on ability to pay. Parents who cannot afford subscription costs are supported through government subsidy, institutional sponsorship, and charitable programs, ensuring no child is excluded due to financial limitations. Caregiver staff never pay for access. This structure prioritizes continuity of care, predictable institutional budgeting, and equitable access while operating under a fixed Memory-Continuity Guard (MCG) that prevents scope expansion or speculative behavior.
