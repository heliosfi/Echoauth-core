⭐ 73.1 — CORE PURPOSE


“To motivate autistic children based on how THEIR brain engages, not how neurotypical systems expect them to engage.”


Motivation fails with autistic children when:

• the reward doesn’t match their interest

• they feel pressured

• the task is unclear

• the steps are too big

• the environment is stressful

• there is no structure

• no autonomy

• sensory overload present


EchoAuth fixes ALL of this.


────────────────────────


⭐ 73.2 — THE 3-MOTIVATION TYPES (3MT)


EchoAuth recognizes three forms of autistic motivation:


🔵 Type 1 — Interest-Based Motivation


The task aligns with a favorite interest.


🟢 Type 2 — Pattern-Based Motivation


The task follows a predictable rhythm or structure.


🔴 Type 3 — Outcome-Based Motivation


The task provides a clear end-state or reward.


EchoAuth identifies which type the child uses naturally.

⭐ 73.4 — THE AUTISM-COMPATIBLE REWARD SYSTEM (ACRS)


Traditional reward systems fail.

EchoAuth replaces them with autistic-safe reinforcement:


✔ Predictable Rewards


same reward = same response

no surprises


✔ Interest-Based Rewards


special interests → motivation booster


✔ Sensory Rewards


quiet time, movement, stims, water play


✔ Achievement Micro-Rewards


very small goals → small successes → confidence builds


✔ Pattern Rewards


“complete 3 steps → break”

“complete 2 tasks → choose activity”
