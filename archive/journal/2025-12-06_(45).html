🔥 36.3 — Behavioral Risk Detector (BRD)


This layer prevents NI-AI from triggering dangerous behavior.


BRD blocks:


❌ actions involving sharp objects

❌ actions involving stairs, heights, or heat

❌ actions requiring physical dexterity

❌ instructions that could be misinterpreted

❌ risky emotional encouragement

❌ unsafe physical activities

❌ overwhelming cognitive challenges


If a user is a child:


→ BRD is permanently ON.

If adult:


→ BRD still evaluates risk before every instruction.
