⭐ 34.2 EPSEF Core Principles (EP-10)


EchoAuth is governed by ten non-negotiable principles:

EP-2 — No Cameras, No Surveillance


EchoAuth NEVER uses:

• cameras

• microphones

• facial recognition

• video screening


Only non-visual, non-invasive sensors allowed.


EP-3 — Parent/Caregiver Sovereignty


NI (Natural Intellect) is the primary decision-maker.

Caregivers ALWAYS have override rights.


EP-4 — Minimal Data Collection


Only what is essential for safety and support is collected.


EP-5 — Local-First Processing


All sensitive analysis (movement, emotion, sensory state) happens locally on the device when possible.


EP-6 — No Data Sharing Without Permission


Schools, clinics, or third parties must request explicit caregiver consent.


EP-7 — Medical Safety Priority


All medical-related actions must default to the safest response.


EP-8 — Environment-Specific Boundaries


Different environments have different privacy limits.


EP-9 — Transparent Operation


EchoAuth must ALWAYS be explainable to caregivers and professionals.


EP-10 — Zero Exploitation Policy


Data can never be used for advertising, profiling, or commercial targeting.
