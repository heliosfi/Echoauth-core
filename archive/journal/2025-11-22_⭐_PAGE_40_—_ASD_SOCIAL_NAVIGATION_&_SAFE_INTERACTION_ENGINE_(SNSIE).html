──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 40.1 — THE CORE PRINCIPLE: “SOCIAL SAFETY FIRST”


EchoAuth teaches:


✔ social safety before social success

✔ comfort before correctness

✔ clarity before complexity

✔ predictability before performance


This prevents overwhelm, masking, and meltdown cycles.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 40.2 — ASD SOCIAL ZONE MAP (SZM)


Three simple social zones:


⭐ Zone 1 — Comfortable Zone


People the child feels safe with.

Calm interactions.

Predictable environments.


⭐ Zone 2 — Cautious Zone


Unfamiliar people.

New environments.

Moderate sensory load.


⭐ Zone 3 — High-Risk Zone


Crowds.

Loud places.

Fast conversations.

Unpredictable people.


EchoAuth adapts behavior rules depending on the zone.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 40.3 — THE SOCIAL PREPARATION ROUTINE (SPR)


Before entering a social setting, EchoAuth helps the child:


✔ understand what will happen

✔ predict the environment

✔ choose comfort objects

✔ practice safe scripts

✔ choose exit strategies

✔ check sensory needs


This PREVENTS meltdowns before they start.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 40.4 — SAFE INTERACTION TEMPLATE (SIT)


EchoAuth gives ASD-friendly scripts for interactions:


✔ “Hi.”

✔ “I need a moment.”

✔ “I don’t know yet.”

✔ “Please wait.”

✔ “Yes / No.”


Short, safe, reliable, zero pressure.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 40.5 — CONVERSATION BREAKDOWN ENGINE (CBE)


EchoAuth breaks conversations into literal steps:

1. They talk.

2. You listen.

3. Pause.

4. You respond.

5. Done.


No hidden rules.

No guessing.

No emotional decoding.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 40.6 — SOCIAL OVERLOAD DETECTION (SOD)


EchoAuth identifies early overload signals:


✔ zoning out

✔ fidgeting increasing

✔ eye contact dropping

✔ voice becoming soft

✔ sudden silence

✔ body turning away

✔ repetitive movements

✔ facial tension


Then triggers:


🔥 “Time for a break?”

🔥 “Need space?”

🔥 “Want quiet?”


EchoAuth protects the child’s nervous system before shutdown.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 40.7 — SOCIAL EXIT ROUTINES (SER)


EchoAuth teaches simple, safe exits:


✔ “I need space.”

✔ “One moment.”

✔ “I’ll be back.”


OR

EchoAuth signals the caregiver privately:


— “He’s overwhelmed.”

— “She needs a break.”

— “It’s time to step out.”


This prevents escalation and embarrassment.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 40.8 — FRIENDSHIP RECOGNITION MAP (FRM)


EchoAuth helps the child identify:


✔ who is kind

✔ who is confusing

✔ who is unsafe

✔ who drains energy

✔ who respects boundaries


Literal, stable, easy-to-understand categories.


──────────────────────────────────


⭐ 40.9 — SOCIAL NAVIGATION SAFETY RULES (SNSR)


EchoAuth teaches:


✔ no one can touch you without asking

✔ you don’t have to talk if you don’t want to

✔ you can take space

✔ you can say “stop”

✔ you can leave anytime


This protects the child in all environments.
