Title:

EchoAuth™ — A Safe, Affordable Authentication System for Non-Verbal Autistic Children & Their Caregivers


Founder:

Nicholas B. Carty, HeliosFi LLC


Loan Request:

$15,000


Purpose of Funding:

To complete the development, testing, and deployment of EchoAuth™, a biometric + behavioral authentication system designed to protect non-verbal autistic children by ensuring the right caregiver is always the one interacting with the child.


The funds will directly support:

• Finalizing the AI safety kernel

• Completing the EchoAuth mobile + web interface

• Building the caregiver training module

• Securing compliance, licensing prep, and deployment tools

• Pilot-testing EchoAuth with ASD families and partner organizations


This loan does not fund personal expenses.

It goes directly into delivery of the EchoAuth system so we can scale into schools, daycares, clinical environments, and home-care settings.


Why EchoAuth Matters:

Millions of non-verbal autistic children cannot say:

“This is not my caregiver.”

EchoAuth solves that problem.


It verifies identity using:

• Biometric signals (touch, motion, voice patterns)

• Behavioral signature (routine, pressure, rhythm)

• AI safety spine (S-modules ensuring non-drift, non-bias, and alignment)


Affordability Promise:

We designed payment so that struggling families are not burdened.


• Parents who can afford it pay the normal subscription

• Low-income families get an affordable access program

• Daycares, schools, and clinical care providers pay a one-time onboarding fee

• Sports teams and influencers can use kinetic-energy wearables to donate access to families who can’t afford it

• No child is ever denied protection due to financial hardship


Expected Impact:

EchoAuth creates safer transitions between:

• Home → School

• Caregiver → Parent

• Therapy → Transportation

• Daycare → After-school programs


The system strengthens trust, protects vulnerable children, and supports caregivers who need real tools to operate safely.


How Revenue Will Be Generated (Loan Repayment Plan):

EchoAuth has three predictable revenue streams to repay Kiva lenders smoothly:

1. Caregiver onboarding fee (training + ID calibration)

2. Parent subscription tier

3. Institutional licensing (schools, daycares, clinics)


Additionally, kinetic-energy wearables allow athletes, influencers, and partners to fund subscriptions for families who qualify for financial relief.


The loan will be repaid using revenue from early institutional partnerships and onboarding fees from pilot sites.
