🧪 GO / NO-GO GATES (THIS IS THE IMPORTANT PART)


You do not apply unless the gate is green.


⸻


✅ GATE 1 — ARCHITECTURE READINESS


GO if ALL are true:

• Default-deny execution enforced

• Authority separation documented

• Non-verbal interaction explicitly supported

• Wearables bounded (no control, no inference)

• Audit logs functional


NO-GO if:

Any decision depends on prediction, interpretation, or verbal confirmation.


⸻


✅ GATE 2 — LANGUAGE ALIGNMENT


GO if ALL are true:

• No outcome claims

• No engagement metrics

• “Non-action as success” stated

• Pricing language locked

• Non-verbal intent explicit


NO-GO if:

Proposal language drifts into optimization, improvement, or behavior change.


⸻


✅ GATE 3 — PILOT READINESS


GO if ALL are true:

• Pilot contract language finalized

• Termination clause clean

• Caregiver onboarding finite

• Parents not pressured to participate


NO-GO if:

Pilot requires proving effectiveness instead of safety.


⸻


✅ GATE 4 — BUDGET INTEGRITY


GO if ALL are true:

• Funds map to safety, compliance, validation

• No predictive modeling line items

• Wearables limited to pilot scale

• Evaluation focuses on logs & restraint


NO-GO if:

Budget rewards activation, usage, or escalation.
