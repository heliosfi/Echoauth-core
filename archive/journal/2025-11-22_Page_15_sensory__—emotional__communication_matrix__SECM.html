⭐ 15.1 — THE THREE INPUT STREAMS


Before EchoAuth speaks, NI–AI checks:


A. Sensory Load Stream (SLS)


Levels 1–5 (regulated → shutdown risk)


B. Emotional Gradient Stream (EGS)


Calm → Uncertain → Distressed → Overwhelmed → Silent


C. Communication Mode Stream (CMS)


Structured → Simplified → Grounded → Safe → Minimal


These streams are cross-referenced instantly.
