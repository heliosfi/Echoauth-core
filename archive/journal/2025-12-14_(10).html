EXECUTIVE FUNDING SUMMARY


NI-AI × EchoAuth

Founder: Nicholas B. Carty

Company: Helios F.I LLC

Location: Mount Vernon, NY


⸻


1. Business Overview


Helios F.I LLC is developing NI-AI × EchoAuth, a governance-first communication and authentication platform designed to support non-verbal and neurodivergent children, their caregivers, and institutions (schools, clinics, community programs).


The system is intentionally non-autonomous, fail-closed, and caregiver-mediated, prioritizing safety, clarity, and trust over automation.


This funding request supports final deployment readiness, pilot operations, and revenue activation.


⸻


2. Problem Statement (CDFI/Kiva Framing)


Non-verbal children and neurodivergent individuals are routinely excluded from digital systems because:

• Most tools assume verbal confirmation

• AI systems escalate or infer incorrectly

• Caregivers lack safe, auditable control

• Institutions cannot adopt tools that feel unsafe or opaque


Existing solutions either:

• Over-automate (introducing risk), or

• Under-support (leaving caregivers without tools)


This gap creates real harm and missed institutional adoption.


⸻


3. The Solution (Plain Language)


NI-AI × EchoAuth provides:

• Non-verbal communication support

• Caregiver-controlled interaction gating

• Role-based authentication without passwords

• Explainable, auditable system behavior


Key principle:


When the system is uncertain, it does nothing and defers to the caregiver.


This makes the platform deployable, fundable, and institution-safe.
