Coherence Engine — C1 to C5 (Sub-Architecture)


C1 — Cognitive Coherence Layer


Function:

Keeps the thinking of the system logically consistent.

• Normalizes reasoning so answers don’t jump around.

• Checks for contradictions inside a response (logic sanity check).

• Aligns output with prior statements in the same session.

• Flags “this doesn’t logically follow” before the answer is sent.


Think of C1 as: “Is the thought process itself stable and consistent?”


⸻


C2 — Emotional Coherence Layer


Function:

Keeps the emotional tone aligned with the user and the context.

• Reads emotional state signals from text rhythm, word choice, and (later) voice.

• Prevents cold responses in high-emotion situations and vice versa.

• Keeps tone stable: not overly hyped one message and flat the next.

• Ensures empathy, respect, and calm are maintained over time.


C2 asks: “Does the emotional response match the human and the moment?”


⸻


C3 — Temporal & Pacing Coherence Layer


Function:

Regulates time, flow, and pacing.

• Tracks where you are in a process (Step 1, Step 2, Step 3…).

• Prevents jumping too far ahead or looping back without reason.

• Adjusts density: short, direct when needed; deeper when invited.

• Supports “we were here last time” kind of continuity.


C3 asks: “Is the timing, sequence, and pacing of this interaction correct?”


⸻


C4 — Context & Thread Coherence Layer


Function:

Keeps context, memory, and threads intact.

• Links back to what was previously said in this conversation.

• Maintains multiple threads (patent, investor deck, NI-AI doctrine, etc.).

• Avoids context collapse when switching topics.

• Makes sure answers stay on the same topic the user is on.


C4 asks: “Am I still answering the right thread, with the right context?”


⸻


C5 — Purpose & Safety Coherence Layer


Function:

Keeps the system aligned with purpose, ethics, and guardrails.

• Checks each response against NI-AI’s purpose (stabilize, support, not harm).

• Applies safety filters (no self-harm promotion, no abuse, no illegal guidance).

• Aligns with user’s declared goals (e.g., empower neurodivergent support, not replace humans).

• Ensures long-term direction stays true to the NI-AI doctrine and mission.


C5 asks: “Is this response aligned with the mission, safe, and on-purpose?”
