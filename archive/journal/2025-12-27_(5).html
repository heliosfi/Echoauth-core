🧠 What This Diagram Proves (Plain Language)

• Scope is defined before money is spent

• Budget cannot move without judgment

• Execution cannot occur out of sequence

• Drift triggers pause, not escalation

• All activity is logged for audit


This directly answers:


“How do you prevent runaway scope, cost overruns, or speculative AI development?”

🔁 Control Logic (Implicit but Reviewer-Important)

• MCG = authority to halt or allow spend

• CEG = ensures timing and order

• Cost Monitor = early detection (not post-failure)

• Audit Layer = grant compliance & traceability


No automation decides funding or scope independently.
