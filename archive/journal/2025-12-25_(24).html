SniperBot’s stance on auto-trading

• Wait

• Governance intact (MCG)


⸻


Is auto-trade possible at all?


Yes — but only in a very narrow, explicit, opt-in mode.


Conditional Auto-Execution (Advanced / Optional)


Auto-trade is allowed only when all are true:

1. Market regime is already classified (e.g., expansion trend day)

2. Entry type is pre-approved (e.g., break-and-retest only)

3. Risk % is fixed and low

4. Max trades per day is capped (often 1)

5. Hard kill-switch is active

6. Human has explicitly armed auto-mode for that session


If any condition breaks → auto-mode shuts off immediately.


This is not “set and forget.”

It’s “armed and supervised.”

The philosophy in one line


Automation handles execution, not judgment.


Judgment remains human.

Automation enforces discipline.
