────────────────────────


⭐ 23.1 Overview


The EchoAuth School Hub (ESH) is a classroom-safe, privacy-compliant, non-camera, multi-sensor device that:

• monitors sensory load

• detects early behavior changes

• identifies emotional shifts

• predicts overload or shutdown

• provides teachers with simplified guidance

• synchronizes with the child’s wearable

• integrates with school routines and transitions

• communicates nonverbally for the child

• protects the child during high-risk school periods


ESH is the FIRST school-centered system built specifically for neurodivergent children.

ESH includes child-safe, FERPA-compliant sensors:


A. Non-Camera Motion Radar


Detects:

• pacing

• freezing

• rocking

• collapse patterns

• agitation increases

• elopement signals


B. Sound Exposure Sensor (Not a microphone)


Measures:

• noise levels

• frequency spikes

C. Light/Visual Overstim Sensor


Detects:

• flicker

• brightness increase

• rapid visual motion

• projector/board glare


D. Environmental Sensors


Measures:

• temperature

• humidity

• CO₂ buildup (affects behavior)


E. Wearable Integration (EWSB)


Reads:

• emotional state

• medical cues

• sensory overload indicators

• nonverbal communication signals
