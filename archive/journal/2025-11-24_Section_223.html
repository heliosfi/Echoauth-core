🔥 223.1 — Intent Verification Layer (IVL-223)


Before executing any action, IVL performs a 3-point intent check:

1. Explicit Intent

– What the user literally said

2. Implicit Intent

– Emotional + cognitive signals hidden beneath the words

3. Contextual Intent

– What the user intended based on previous steps

– Purpose Thread from TLE

– Emotional State Engine signals


If there is any mismatch, the system pauses and asks:


“Do you want me to proceed?”
