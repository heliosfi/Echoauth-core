Dear Lending Committee,


I am writing to submit this application on behalf of EchoAuth, an early-stage, NYC-based technology project designed to support non-verbal and neurodivergent individuals and their caregivers through safe, governed access and interaction systems.


EchoAuth was intentionally developed with a safety-first and governance-first approach. Rather than relying on automated decision-making or assumptions, the system uses a deterministic governance layer—Melanin Cognitive Governance (MCG v3.0)—to determine when it is appropriate to proceed, pause, or regulate an action. This design ensures that technology supports caregivers and families without overriding human authority or introducing unnecessary risk.


At this stage, we are seeking CDFI financing to support:

• completion of a working prototype,

• validation of safety and governance logic, and

• small, controlled pilot deployments with community or institutional partners.


The requested funds will be used exclusively for project execution, safety validation, and early pilot readiness. The project has been structured to operate sustainably at a modest scale, with conservative revenue assumptions and clear repayment pathways tied to pilot contracts, subscriptions, and service agreements. Repayment is not dependent on rapid scaling or speculative growth.


EchoAuth aligns closely with community development priorities by:

• improving access for underserved and neurodivergent populations,

• reducing barriers caused by verbal-only or rigid systems,

• supporting caregivers with safer, more transparent tools, and

• prioritizing ethical and responsible technology deployment.


We have included a concise application-safe package outlining:

• the project’s purpose and community impact,

• safety and clinical considerations,

• use of funds and repayment logic, and

• execution readiness.


Our goal is to build responsibly, validate carefully, and grow in alignment with community needs and lender expectations. We welcome the opportunity to work with [Lender Name] and appreciate your consideration of this application.


Thank you for your time and for your continued support of community-focused enterprises.
