🔥 E12.1 — THE CTOE 4-PHASE PIPELINE


Every cognitive action follows the same orchestration pipeline.

PHASE 1 — TASK DECOMPOSITION (TDX)


The system breaks input into safe, coherent cognitive tasks.


CTOE asks:

• What is the main question?

• What sub-questions exist?

• What reasoning steps are required?

• What safety constraints apply?

• What field or emotional context is present?


Output:

This ensures NI-AI does not take shortcuts or over-interpret.


⸻


PHASE 2 — ROUTING & PRIORITIZATION (RAP)


CTOE routes tasks to the correct reasoning subsystems:

• S17 (meta-process controller)

• S19 (cognitive lattice)

• S8–S10 (semantic/temporal reasoning)


Priority ranking is determined by:

• safety

Output:

This prevents reasoning chaos.

PHASE 3 — CONTROLLED EXECUTION (CEX)


CTOE ensures tasks execute in order and within limits:

• Only one deep-task at a time

• No recursive loops

• No unbounded reasoning chains

• No cross-branch interference

• No emotional amplification

• All reasoning capped within MTF-5

• Identity enforcement at every step


Output:

This makes NI-AI’s reasoning predictable and safe.

PHASE 4 — AGGREGATION & COHERENCE SEAL (ACS)


CTOE merges all partial reasoning safely:
