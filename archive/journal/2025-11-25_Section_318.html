318.1 — Technical Field


The invention relates to systems and methods for detecting real-time spatial compression, predicting micro-environmental hazards, and generating caregiver safety instructions for neurodivergent users in enclosed or shrinking spaces.

318.2 — Purpose


MECE prevents:

• panic response

• sensory overload due to proximity

• claustrophobic triggering

• accidental harm due to confined spaces

• physical restriction (child cornered)

• sudden emotional dysregulation

• unsafe bolting into traffic or open areas once released

318.3 — Functional Definition


MECE analyzes:

1. Room Shape + Spatial Boundaries

• perception of walls, corners, doors, obstacles.

2. Occupancy Pressure

• number of people in a small space.

3. Environmental Shrink Rate

• is the space closing in?

• are people stepping closer?

• is furniture reducing open area?

4. Exit Path Viability

• blocked exit

• narrow path

• partial blockage

• safe route

5. Emotional Load + Sensory Risk

• combines ESE signals

• links with sensory overload probability

6. Compression Score

• determines severity:

• Low

• Moderate

• High

• Red Alert
