PROJECT NAME:

EchoAuth™ — A Biometric Safety & Communication System for Non-Verbal Autistic Children


LOAN PURPOSE (What this Kiva loan will fund):

I am requesting this Kiva loan to complete the final development stages of EchoAuth™, a safety-critical biometric system built for non-verbal and semi-verbal autistic children. These funds will cover the last technical components needed for deployment:

• Secure backend development

• Caregiver onboarding portal

• Child safety interface

• Wearable-sensor integration

• MVP certification and compliance


Once complete, EchoAuth will allow parents, caregivers, and institutions to reliably verify a child’s emotional state, safety status, and communication needs — all through a validated, non-invasive system.


WHY THIS MATTERS:

Millions of autistic children struggle with communication barriers that lead to safety risks, emotional overwhelm, or misunderstanding in school, daycare, and caregiving environments. EchoAuth is designed to give:

• Parents peace of mind

• Caregivers a clear safety framework

• Institutions a reliable compliance tool

• Children a protected way to express needs without pressure or fear


This Kiva loan accelerates the final build so we can begin deploying EchoAuth into communities that need it most.


⸻


REVENUE MODEL (Affordability-Focused and Non-Burdening)


EchoAuth is designed so that no struggling family is ever financially blocked from receiving protection.


1. Families (Parents):

• Parents who can afford it choose a monthly subscription for continuous safety monitoring and communication support.

• Low-income families access reduced-cost, assisted plans so finances never create a barrier.


2. Independent Caregivers (non-clinical, non-institutional):

• Babysitters, nannies, respite workers, or family friends pay a one-time onboarding fee only.

• No recurring subscription is required for them.

• This makes caregiver access widely available without creating long-term financial strain.


3. Institutions (Daycare, Schools, Clinical Centers):

• These organizations pay a standard subscription fee, which is:

• Tax-deductible

• Often reimbursable through early-childhood or special-education budgets

• EchoAuth improves compliance, staff safety training, and real-time awareness in classrooms and daycare environments.


⸻


COMMUNITY SUPPORT THROUGH WEARABLES:


EchoAuth integrates with optional kinetic-energy wearables.

When worn, movement generates micro-donations that help:

• Subsidize low-income families

• Support special-needs programs

• Sponsor free EchoAuth access for families in crisis


Professional athletes, runners, and wellness organizations will have the option to wear these devices as part of a community-support initiative — transforming everyday motion into direct assistance.

SOCIAL IMPACT:


EchoAuth directly supports:

• Autistic children who struggle to express basic needs

• Caregivers who require structure and clarity

• Daycare and school systems that must meet safety standards

• Communities seeking better tools for vulnerable children


This technology reduces miscommunication, improves safety, and builds trust between families and the professionals who care for them.
