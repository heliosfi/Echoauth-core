⭐ 33.3 Core Function of CARE


CARE answers one question:


“What should EchoAuth do RIGHT NOW, given the child’s state AND the environment they are in?”


To do this, CARE:

1. Classifies the environment

2. Measures risk level

3. Determines decision tier (from EDT)

4. Identifies the correct device pathway

5. Adjusts output based on location safety rules

6. Verifies NI overrides

7. Executes the action

⭐ 33.8 NI-Overridable vs Non-Overridable Actions


CARE respects NI–AI rules:


NI can override:

• tone selection

• color scheme

• intervention type

• duration of calming sequence

• preference-based actions

• transition strategy


NI CANNOT override:

• safety rules

• medical escalations

• CPR-level risks

• elopement alerts

• collapse detection

• fever/hydration emergency markers


This protects the child and protects you legally.
