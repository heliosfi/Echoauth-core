1. What problem are you solving?


Today, over 30 million autistic and neurodivergent children worldwide struggle with one simple issue:


➡️ They cannot communicate their needs in a safe, trusted, consistent way.


Families, schools, and caregivers spend hours trying to interpret:

• What the child wants

• Whether they feel safe

• When they need help

• Who is allowed to interact with them


Traditional apps fail because they rely on:

• language-heavy interfaces

• unpredictable AI behavior

• complex learning curves

• lack of structure

• unsafe model outputs


There is no system that gives non-verbal children a protected channel to express themselves.


EchoAuth exists to change that.


⸻


2. What is EchoAuth?


EchoAuth is the world’s first neuro-support authentication system built for autistic and non-verbal children.


Instead of relying on passwords or biometrics, EchoAuth uses:

• structured communication grids

• NI-AI (Natural Intelligence + AI) safety layers

• caregiver override channels

• zero-emotion, zero-risk model behavior

• predictable, repeatable interactions


It is not a chatbot

It is not a typical AI app


EchoAuth is a communication safety system.


It gives children a way to express:

• Yes / No

• Needs

• Discomfort

• Safety alerts

• Environment changes

• Emotional state


Inside a closed, protected environment designed for autism.
