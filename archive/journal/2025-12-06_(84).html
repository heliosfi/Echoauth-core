🔥 The Problem


Millions of neurodivergent children — especially nonverbal autistic children — face a silent crisis:

• They cannot reliably express needs, distress, or urgency

• Caregivers cannot verify emotional state or intent

• Digital systems cannot adapt to sensory overload

• Traditional authentication systems assume verbal, cognitive, or motor skills many ASD users do not have

• Schools, homes, clinics, and community centers lack safe tools that regulate interactions between caregivers and children


Current technology is not designed for the neurodivergent mind.


This leads to:

• Miscommunication

• Safety risks

• Emotional meltdowns

• Caregiver burnout

• Lost autonomy

• Limited ability to engage in digital learning or health systems


There is no coherent, field-aware, role-aware authentication system built for ASD users.


EchoAuth fills this gap.
