Subject: Inquiry re: Phase I SBIR Fit — Governed AI Decision-Support for Non-Verbal Interaction


Dear Dr. [Last Name],


I hope this message finds you well. I am writing to request brief guidance regarding the programmatic fit of a proposed Phase I SBIR application currently in preparation by Helios F.I LLC.


Our proposed project evaluates a governance-first AI decision-support system designed to operate safely in non-verbal, caregiver-mediated contexts, where conventional AI systems often escalate or infer beyond their authority.


The system—NI-AI, integrated with EchoAuth and governed by MCG (Motion / Context Governance)—is architected to:

• Separate observation, authorization, and execution

• Default to pause and non-action under uncertainty

• Provide explainable, auditable outputs to caregivers and institutions

• Avoid autonomous decision-making, diagnosis, or behavioral prediction


Phase I research focuses exclusively on technical evaluation of system stability, restraint, explainability, and auditability using simulated and anonymized interaction scenarios. No clinical deployment, diagnosis, or human-subjects research is proposed in Phase I.


The work aligns with NIH priorities in:

• Trustworthy and explainable AI

• Human-centered and caregiver-supported technologies

• Ethical AI design in sensitive environments


Before final submission, I would appreciate your guidance on whether this scope appears appropriate for [Institute / Program Name], or if you would recommend an alternative NIH program or study section.


Thank you for your time and consideration. I would be glad to provide a brief summary or speak at your convenience.
