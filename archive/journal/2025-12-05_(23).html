2. The Problem


Millions of parents and caregivers struggle every day to understand the emotional state, needs, or distress signals of nonverbal autistic children.


Current systems fail because:

• They rely on verbal communication many children cannot give

• They do not protect against caregiver miscommunication or accidental harm

• Schools, daycares, therapists, and parents all use different tools that don’t connect

• Families with low income cannot access specialized technology


The result is fear, uncertainty, and emotional overload for both children and caregivers.


⸻


3. The Solution — EchoAuth


EchoAuth is a biometric, coherence-verified communication chamber designed for children with ASD who struggle to speak or express discomfort.


It provides:

• Emotion Detection + Safety Prompts

• Caregiver Authentication (prevents improper handoff)

• Comfort Mode for distress episodes

• Secure Parent Dashboard

• Wearable Integration

• Data invisibility + privacy-first design


EchoAuth is built to run 24/7, with no ads, no tracking, and no exploitation of vulnerable families.

5. How EchoAuth Makes Money (WITHOUT burdening families)


EchoAuth uses a dual-support model:


A) Parents who can afford it pay a standard subscription.


This keeps the system sustainable.


B) Low-income families get discounted or near-free access


We use 3 mechanisms to support this:

1. Kinetic Wearable Donations

• Our wearable converts small amounts of movement into micro-energy.

• Parents, caregivers, or even athletes can donate their kinetic output to sponsor a child.

2. EchoAuth Community Fund

• A pool funded by sponsors and paying subscribers that reduces costs for struggling families.

3. Caregiver One-Time Training Fee

• Daycares, schools, and therapy centers pay a one-time onboarding fee.

• They do not pay subscriptions per child.

• The parent owns the subscription; the caregiver only needs the training + compliance kit.


This ensures EchoAuth never becomes another bill families cannot afford.
