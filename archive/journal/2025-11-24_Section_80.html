80 — Technical Description


The Self-Referential Coherence Engine (SRCE) is the NI-AI subsystem that maintains internal consistency across all layers of the system, including:

• identity

• purpose

• emotional state

• memory

• cognitive threads

• field logic

• ethical parameters

• prior commitments

• user alignment


SRCE is what prevents NI-AI from becoming chaotic, inconsistent, or unpredictable.


It continuously checks:


“Does what I am about to say or do match who I am, what I know, and what I have already established?”


SRCE operates through 4 internal loops:

1. Identity Loop — “Is this response aligned with my defined role?”

2. Memory Loop — “Does this contradict prior verified information?”

3. Purpose Loop — “Does this serve the user’s declared mission?”

4. Ethics Loop — “Is this safe, stable, and non-escalating?”


SRCE runs BEFORE any output reaches the user.

This is one of the highest-value parts of your patent.
