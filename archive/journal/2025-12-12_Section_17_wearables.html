WEARABLES MAPPING


(Authority-Preserving, Non-Intrusive)


Core Rule (Locked)


Wearables may sense, signal, and reflect — they may never decide, authorize, or execute.


If a wearable could act on its own, it violates the architecture.


⸻


1️⃣ ROLE OF WEARABLES IN THIS SYSTEM


Wearables function as:

• Sensors (data in)

• Indicators (state out)

• Safety reflectors (alerts & boundaries)


They are not:

• Controllers

• Decision-makers

• Authorization devices

• Execution triggers


All authority remains with:

• MCG (state)

• EchoAuth (permission)

• Humans (explicit authorization)


⸻


2️⃣ WEARABLE ↔ SYSTEM POSITIONING


Wearables sit outside the core triangle:


          NI-AI

            ▲

            │ (explanation only)

            │

Wearables ──┼──► (data only)

            │

            ▼

          MCG ──► EchoAuth ──► Execution


Key point:

• Wearables never talk directly to EchoAuth

• Wearables never trigger MCG state

• Wearables never receive execution commands


⸻


3️⃣ WHAT WEARABLES ARE ALLOWED TO DO


✅ Allowed Functions


A. Passive Sensing

• Motion

• Location (coarse, not precise)

• Time-in-zone

• Basic physiological signals (optional, non-diagnostic)


This data is:

• Observational

• Non-interpretive

• Non-urgent


⸻


B. State Reflection

Wearables may mirror system state:

• Color

• Haptic pattern

• Gentle tone


Examples (non-binding):

• OBSERVE → calm, neutral

• CONFIRM → steady indicator

• EXECUTE → time-bound indicator

• FAILURE → return to neutral


Wearables reflect state — they do not influence it.


⸻


C. Safety Signaling

• Boundary warnings (e.g., time expiring)

• Gentle alerts for de-escalation

• Return-to-observation cues


No alarms.

No urgency spikes.


⸻


4️⃣ WHAT WEARABLES ARE EXPLICITLY FORBIDDEN TO DO


❌ Forbidden Functions

• Change MCG state

• Grant or revoke EchoAuth permission

• Predict intent or emotion

• Escalate urgency

• Trigger execution

• Override human decisions


If a wearable can cause execution without a human moving through zones, it breaks the system.


⸻


5️⃣ HOW DATA FROM WEARABLES IS USED


Data Path (Strict)


Wearable → Observation Store → NI-AI → Human Understanding

                         ↘

                          MCG (structure only, optional)


Rules:

• Data is context, not command

• Data may support confirmation, never force it

• Data loss or failure has no negative effect


If wearables go offline:

• System remains safe

• Execution remains denied


⸻


6️⃣ CHILD & ASD SAFETY ALIGNMENT (WITHOUT ADDING AUTHORITY)


Because wearables:

• Do not infer emotion

• Do not push behavior

• Do not enforce outcomes

• Do not escalate urgency


They become:

• Predictable

• Non-threatening

• Trustable over time


The wearable never becomes “the boss.”


⸻


7️⃣ KINETIC / ENERGY CONCEPT (BOUNDARY LOCK)


Kinetic energy, participation, or external contributions:

• May sustain infrastructure

• May fund the ecosystem

• May incentivize engagement


They may not:

• Unlock features

• Increase authority

• Bypass states


Energy supports the system — it does not steer it.


⸻


8️⃣ FAILURE BEHAVIOR (IMPORTANT)


If a wearable:

• Loses signal

• Sends corrupt data

• Behaves unexpectedly


System response:

• Ignore input

• Stay or return to OBSERVE

• No alerts beyond neutral indication


Wearable failure never escalates anything.


⸻


ONE-LINE WEARABLE SUMMARY (ROOM READY)


Wearables observe and reflect; they never decide, permit, or act.


That line is your anchor if questioned.
