🔥 PAGE 85 — MEDICAL EARLY WARNING & HEALTH RECORD ENGINE (MEWHR-E)


“How EchoAuth protects autistic children by monitoring health, storing patterns, and giving early alerts when something is wrong.”

────────────────────────


⭐ 85.1 — CORE PURPOSE


The biggest danger for nonverbal or partially verbal autistic children:

• They can’t describe internal pain

• Doctors often misjudge symptoms

• Parents may not know early signs

• Medical emergencies are caught TOO LATE


EchoAuth becomes a medical intelligence layer that:


✔ tracks health

✔ detects early warning signs

✔ logs eating/drinking patterns

✔ stores symptoms

✔ supports parents during emergencies

✔ gives doctors REAL information

✔ prevents dehydration, fever, infections, and slow organ distress


No parent should watch their child suffer because “they couldn’t explain it.”


EchoAuth becomes the voice.


────────────────────────


⭐ 85.2 — HEALTH SIGNAL MONITORING (HSM-6)


EchoAuth tracks 6 key health indicators:


1️⃣ Temperature patterns

– early fever

– cold spells

– unusual fluctuations


2️⃣ Hydration signals

– dry lips

– shallow breath

– low energy

– reduced bathroom frequency


3️⃣ Pain indicators (nonverbal)

– winces

– posture guarding

– avoiding touch

– curled body

– inconsistent walking


4️⃣ Energy level changes

– sudden fatigue

– low activity

– no appetite

– hollow eyes


5️⃣ Fluid intake

– how much water per day

– dehydration risk score


6️⃣ Digestive patterns

– appetite

– bowel movement frequency

– food reactions


This is not “medical advice.”

This is pattern intelligence.


────────────────────────


⭐ 85.3 — PAIN & DISCOMFORT DETECTION ENGINE (PDDE)


Autistic children express pain differently.


EchoAuth analyzes:


✔ micro-expressions

✔ sudden stillness

✔ flinching

✔ self-protective posture

✔ unusual stimming

✔ breath irregularity

✔ slow-down movement


This allows detection of:

• stomach pain

• headache

• infection

• ear pain

• kidney discomfort

• dehydration

• fever onset

• sensory illness response


Your son’s kidney dehydration case would’ve been detected.

A secure vault storing:

• all medical visits

• allergies

• medication list

• food reactions

• pain episodes

• hydration emergencies

• doctor notes

• patterns across months/years

• heat sensitivity or cold sensitivity

• sensory medical triggers


Doctors won’t have to “guess.”


EchoAuth provides the pattern timeline.

⭐ 85.5 — NUTRITION PATTERN LOGGER (NPL)


The system automatically tracks:


✔ what the child eats

✔ how often

✔ how regularly

✔ preferred foods

✔ nutrition risks

✔ hydration vs. dry foods

✔ weekly variety

✔ reactions to certain foods

✔ appetite changes


This is CRITICAL for:

• kidney health

• dehydration

• digestive safety

• blood sugar stability

• immune system

• sensory-based eating behaviors


A clear weekly food dataset means the parent NEVER has to guess.
