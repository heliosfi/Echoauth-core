🌱 Kiva Borrower Story — NYC Version


Helios F.I LLC | Mount Vernon / NYC Metro


My name is Nicholas B. Carty, and I’m the founder of Helios F.I LLC, a New York–based company building caregiver-mediated, non-verbal support technology for families, schools, and care settings.


This business was born out of lived experience.


As a father and caregiver, I’ve seen firsthand how many children — especially non-verbal and neurodivergent children — are placed under constant pressure to explain, speak, comply, or perform in environments that are already overwhelming. Too often, silence is treated as a problem instead of a form of communication.


Helios F.I exists to change that.


⸻


🧠 What We Do


We are building protective, safety-first technology that supports non-verbal communication by removing the need to speak, explain, or justify behavior.


Our system:

• Does not require speech

• Treats silence and stillness as valid signals

• Keeps caregivers in full control

• Prioritizes safety, predictability, and calm

• Is designed for use in homes, schools, and care environments


This is not surveillance, diagnosis, or automation.

It is infrastructure that creates space — especially for children who are often misunderstood.


⸻


🏙️ Why NYC Matters


New York City has:

• One of the largest populations of neurodivergent children in the country

• A dense network of schools, caregivers, and service providers

• Families who need practical, respectful tools, not experimental tech


Helios F.I is committed to local deployment, community partnerships, and responsible growth rooted in the NYC metro area.
