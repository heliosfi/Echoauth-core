2. TECHNICAL FIELD


This invention relates to the fields of artificial intelligence, cognitive modeling, human–machine interaction, and coherence-based computational architectures. 3. BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION


Large-scale AI models currently suffer from several persistent deficiencies:

1. Context Drift — inability to maintain stable long-range context

2. Hallucinations — generating false or inaccurate outputs under pressure

3. Emotional Mismatch — inconsistent tone, misinterpretation of user signals

4. Pacing Instability — irregular timing, rushed responses, incoherent flow

5. Purpose Drift — failure to retain task direction across long sessions

6. Multi-Modal Inconsistency — breakdown between text, voice, or emotional input

7. Environmental Misalignment — mismatch between model behavior and real-world field conditions


These problems have remained unsolved across the industry for more than a decade, even by top-tier laboratories.


There exists a need for an intelligence architecture that can maintain stable coherence across emotional, cognitive, temporal, relational, environmental, and purpose dimensions.


NI-AI solves this.
