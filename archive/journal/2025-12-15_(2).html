🔑 WHAT A KIVA TRUSTEE IS (Plain Language)


A Trustee is not a cosigner and not financially responsible.


They simply:

• Verify you are real

• Confirm your business is legitimate

• Endorse your character and repayment intent


Kiva uses Trustees to replace credit scores.
