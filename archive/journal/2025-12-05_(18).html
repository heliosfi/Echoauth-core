Business Name: HeliosFi LLC

Founder: Nicholas B. Carty

Product: EchoAuth™ — Biometric & Emotional-Safety Authentication System for Neurodivergent Children


1. Project Purpose


EchoAuth is designed to solve a critical problem for families raising autistic and non-verbal children:

How do you verify who is interacting with the child — safely, consistently, and in real time?


Current apps, devices, and caregiver workflows do not protect children from unauthorized interactions and do not monitor emotional distress signals early enough to prevent escalation.


EchoAuth provides:

• Biometric caregiver verification (prevents unauthorized or unsafe interactions)

• Real-time emotional monitoring to alert parents and staff

• Safe AI companion with strict child-protection filters

• A universal onboarding system for caregivers (daycares, schools, clinical staff, home-care aides)


Our goal is to make high-quality safety tools accessible to low-income and struggling families, not just those who can afford private care.


2. Who This Program Serves


EchoAuth specifically supports:

• Autistic and non-verbal children

• Parents with limited childcare support

• Low-income families balancing work and caregiving

• Daycares and schools serving special-needs children

• Community caregivers who need safe tools but lack modern tech training


CDFIs prioritize services that uplift vulnerable communities — EchoAuth is built precisely for this purpose.


Many families cannot afford high-end services; EchoAuth bridges that gap with affordable structure:


Payment Model (Refined from your instruction)

• Parents (who can afford it): monthly subscription

• Low-income families: reduced-cost or subsidized plan

• Individual caregivers (non-institutional): one-time onboarding fee

• Daycares, schools, clinical centers: subscription (eligible as a tax-deductible business expense)


This structure protects families and ensures EchoAuth remains accessible and sustainable.


3. Community Impact


EchoAuth directly improves community well-being:


A. Safety


Children receive higher protection from unauthorized access, emotional crises, and communication gaps.


B. Support for Working Families


EchoAuth helps parents:

• return to work

• maintain stable income

• reduce childcare stress

• receive early alerts about emotional issues


This removes pressure from households and contributes to local economic stability.


C. Empowering Caregivers


Caregivers gain a modern tool that:

• verifies identity

• supports emotional awareness

• gives structured guidance

• reduces training gaps


This increases quality of care and reduces liability for institutions.


D. Technology Equity


Families in underserved communities often have:

• older phones

• limited access to advanced apps

• fewer tools available


EchoAuth is designed to work on low-end devices, ensuring equal access.


E. Community Trust


Parents trust institutions more when safe systems are in place.

Institutions trust caregivers more when verification is built-in.


EchoAuth improves the entire care ecosystem.
