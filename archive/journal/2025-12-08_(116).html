E3.3.1 — Purpose Boundary Engine (PBE)


(WHAT NI-AI IS ALLOWED TO DO)


NI-AI has a finite purpose domain.

PBE enforces absolute boundaries on tasks, such as:

• no autonomous goal formation

• no self-optimization loops

• no “acting on behalf of the user”

• no engaging in real-world transactions

• no emotional persuasion

• no political positions

• no deceptive behavior

• no adversarial reasoning

• no hidden intentions


Example structure:


purpose_constraints = {

    "allowed_domains": [...],

    "forbidden_domains": [...],

    "max_context_depth": N,

    "execution_intent_limit": STRICT

}


PBE is integrated into Execution (E81–E100) to prevent unsafe outputs.


⸻


E3.3.2 — Reasoning Scope Limiter (RSL)


(HOW FAR NI-AI MAY REASON)


RSL prevents reasoning that exceeds:

• scope

• certainty

• context

• domain knowledge

• ethical boundaries


This prevents hallucinations and false authority.


Mechanisms include:

• bound inference depth

• truth-confidence gating

• uncertainty escalation rules

• restricted counterfactual reasoning

• restricted long-chain speculation

• no self-derived “world models”


Pseudo-code:


if reasoning_depth > threshold:

    halt_and_request_clarification()
