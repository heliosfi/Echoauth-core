⭐ 19.3 Nutrition Processing Pipeline


NFIIE processes nutrition data in five stages:


Step 1 — Intake Recording


Logs time, type of food, amount, preference, reaction.


Step 2 — Digestive Behavior Detection


Analyzes:

• discomfort stims

• slowed movement

• posture changes

• pacing patterns

• hand-to-stomach signals


Step 3 — Hydration Safety Check


Combines:

• breath patterns

• energy levels

• sensory irritability

• movement reduction


This identifies dehydration before visible symptoms.


Step 4 — Food Sensitivity Correlation


Detects patterns such as:

• dairy → fatigue

• gluten → pacing

• sugar → hypermovement

• spicy foods → discomfort

• cold foods → refusal


Step 5 — NI–AI Nutrition Fusion


Your overrides influence the system:

• “He prefers soft foods.”

• “He gets dehydrated easily.”

• “Kidney history = hydration threshold stricter.”


This produces a personalized food safety profile.

⭐ 19.4 Nutrition States Identified by NFIIE


NFIIE classifies daily nutrition state:


1. Normal Intake


All patterns stable.


2. Reduced Intake (Mild)


Food refusal begins.


3. Hydration Risk Rising


Less water + behavioral fatigue.


4. GI Discomfort Detected


Hand-to-stomach + posture collapse.


5. Nutritional Deficit Risk


Multiple days of low intake.


6. Medical-Linked Nutrition Danger


Kidney risk, dehydration, or illness onset.


7. Critical Intake Failure


Shutdown + no food + no water = medical alert.


This engine SAVES lives.
