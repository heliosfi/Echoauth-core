Inputs might be:

• Camera → micro-expressions, gaze, posture

• Mic → tone, pace, volume

• Wearables (optional) → heart rate, movement

• Environmental sensors → light level, EM noise, sound level


Key sub-modules (matching the diagrams you sent):

1. Photon Flow Model

• Inspired by that Monte Carlo photon diagram.

• For us: we don’t simulate physics; we copy the logic:


Capture frame → detect edges/faces →

check "boundary events" (sudden movement, flinch, turn away) →

classify:

  - Reflected  = avoidance / resistance

  - Absorbed   = deep focus / freeze

  - Transmitted = comfortable engagement



2. EM / Rhythm Model

• From the EM brain diagram: we track rhythm + interference.

• For us: use heart-rate variability, motion jitter, voice tremor as a proxy for “signal vs noise.”

3. Non-verbal Cue Extractor

• Posture openness/closed

• Eye contact yes/no

• Fidgeting intensity

• Distance from camera / device
