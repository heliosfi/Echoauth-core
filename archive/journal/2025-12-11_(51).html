🏗 Step 2: Use Style Profile in the Reply


Find _default_text_generation in mirror_agent.py and tweak it slightly:


    def _default_text_generation(self, mc_os: MelaninCognitionOS) -> str:

        data = mc_os.to_dict()

        r = data["role_output"]


        parts = []


        # You can sprinkle a signature line sometimes, or keep it minimal

        parts.append("Short version:\n")


        parts.append("🔎 Navigator (where this is heading):")

        parts.append(r["navigator_direction"] or "(no clear direction yet)")


        parts.append("\n🛡 Guardian (what to watch):")

        parts.append(r["guardian_warnings"] or "(no major flags detected)")


        parts.append("\n🏗 Architect (how to hold this in your mind):")

        parts.append(r["architect_expression"])


        parts.append("\n✅ Next steps (keep it real):")

        parts.append(r["architect_next_steps"])


        # Optional: light Ase stamp at the end

        parts.append("\nAse.\n")


        return "\n".join(parts)
