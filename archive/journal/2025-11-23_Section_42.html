⭐ 42.3 XAIFS Three-Layer Architecture


XAIFS delivers explanations in three levels depending on context:


⸻


Layer 1 — Simple Explanation (for parents & teachers)


Short, clear, plain English.


Examples:

• “He is overwhelmed by noise.”

• “She needs hydration soon.”

• “This environment is too bright.”

• “He is getting tired.”

• “She needs a break.”


⸻


Layer 2 — Technical Summary (for therapists & clinicians)


Detailed, but not overwhelming.


Examples:

• “Elevated sensory load + posture tension indicates rising distress.”

• “Breath patterns suggest mild autonomic stress.”

• “Cognitive load spike detected: task complexity mismatch.”


⸻


Layer 3 — Full Diagnostic Trace (for medical teams & engineering logs)


A complete reason chain.


Example:

• “Noise increase + pacing + breath irregularity = 78% risk of meltdown within 3 minutes. IME triggered, hydration recommended, safe-path activated.”


This is FULL explainability.
