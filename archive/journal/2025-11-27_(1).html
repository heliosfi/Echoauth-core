🧪 IPVP — Institutional Pilot Validation Plan


1. Pilot Objective


Prove system reliability, caregiver identity continuity, single-payload communication routing, trauma-safe tone pacing, and denial-risk-scored export integrity in real-world institutional environments serving non-verbal and neurodivergent children.


⸻


2. Target Population

• 10–20 non-verbal or minimally verbal ASD children in caregiver-authorized settings.

• 10–15 primary caregivers enrolled as platform operators and governance owners.


⸻


3. Pilot Environments (Institutional Targets)


Choose any 2 spaces from this list for deployment lanes:

1. Local public elementary school

2. Community caregiver safe collaboration center

3. Pediatric ER communication desk

4. Institutional caregiver support co-watch trip programs


These environments give real-world validation without medical diagnosis classification.


⸻


4. Pilot Duration

• Total runtime window: 90 days (3 months)

• Daily use cadence: 5–10 interactions per family per day

• Minimum execution cycles logged per week: 35–50 per institution.


⸻


5. Validation Metrics (What You Measure for Success)


Each metric is logged per interaction cycle with timestamp seals:


✔ Identity Confidence Continuity

(no credential re-entry required after first confirmation)


✔ Communication Reliability

(every cycle emits one coherent response only)


✔ Purpose-Context Retention

(no contradiction of user or caregiver intent)


✔ Tone Stability & Load Adaptation

(pacing adjusts to calm or high-stress environments)


✔ Forensic Chain Traceability

(reasoning path can be back-tracked without recomposition)


✔ Denial Risk Score

(classifies if an interaction is eligible for payer or institutional export, before sending outward)


⸻


6. Output Constraints (Safety Rules Enforced in Pilot)


The system must never:


🚫 Output more than one payload per reasoning cycle

🚫 Rewrite caregiver decisions with emotional override

🚫 Infer medical diagnosis

🚫 Claim subatomic particle sensing

🚫 Lose identity continuity between caregivers enrolled

🚫 Present contradicting answers in the same export object

🚫 Export without timestamp + classification validation.


⸻


7. Export Envelope Format for Adoption Proof


All outbound pilot records must be institutional-safe and auditor-ready, structured like:


Parent/Caregiver ID snapshot  

Resolved intent tag  

Single I/O response  

Pacing/tone cadence noted  

Eligibility verified (no recomposition)  

Denial-risk scored  

Timestamp seal appended


Institutions and payers can accept this as proof-of-use or adoption-worth metrics.


⸻


8. Adoption Success Definition


At the end of 90 days, the system is considered pilot-validated if:

• 95%+ of interactions require no recentering or coherence repair

• 0 interactions include multi-payload collision

• 0 interactions generate diagnosis or physics sensing claims

• Caregivers report stress load reduced, not increased

• Institutions confirm communication reliability for neurodivergent child interfaces

• Export envelopes contain no blank or conflicting fields

• Identity never bleeds or resets between caregivers enrolled.


⸻

1. Non-Provisional patent evidence of reliability

3. Institutional adoption testimony for hospital or school partnerships

4. Payer or CDFI frameworks that need denial-free classification models

5. Scaled MVP buildout funding hooks for VC or impact investors.
