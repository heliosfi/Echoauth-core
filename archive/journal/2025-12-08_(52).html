SECTION 4 — PROJECT DESCRIPTION


The EchoAuth + NI-AI runtime consists of five vertical floors:


1. ORI Kernel (Identity Stability)


Ensures predictable tone, behavior, and alignment.

Removes drift and hallucination at the identity level.


2. Interpretation Layer (NI Processor)


Extracts meaning, tone, stress, urgency, and intent.

Designed specifically to model ASD communication.


3. Memory Governance & Flow Control


Manages multi-session tasks, teacher/clinician continuity, and reduces cognitive overload.


4. Field Awareness & Lattice Reasoning


Optional module for robotics and multi-agent systems.

Provides spatial and contextual safety.


5. Coherence + UMCE Execution Safety


Guarantees all outputs are consistent, compliant, ethical, and safe.

Fail-closed behavior prevents unsafe responses.
