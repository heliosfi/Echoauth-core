1. ORGANIZATION & PRODUCT SUMMARY


Organization: EchoAuth

Focus: Assistive technology for non-verbal autistic children and caregivers


EchoAuth provides non-verbal caregiver–child authentication and communication support using biometric signals and role-based continuity safeguards.


The system is designed for use in homes, schools, clinics, and care environments to reduce misidentification, caregiver error, and communication breakdown. EchoAuth prioritizes safety, role authentication, and continuity of care rather than data extraction or experimental AI behavior.


⸻


2. PROBLEM BEING ADDRESSED (LENDER-SAFE)


Non-verbal autistic children often rely on multiple caregivers across home, school, and clinical environments. Breakdowns in caregiver continuity and role verification can lead to miscommunication, delayed care, and safety risks. Existing solutions focus on documentation or behavioral interpretation rather than secure caregiver–child role continuity.


EchoAuth addresses this gap by providing secure, role-based authentication and communication support without emotion inference, surveillance, or speculative AI behavior.


⸻


3. USE OF FUNDS (CDFI / KIVA APPROVED)


Loan proceeds will be used only for fixed, non-speculative expenses:

• Prototype completion and testing

• Assistive hardware and wearable components

• Compliance preparation and certifications

• Pilot deployment in schools, clinics, or caregiver organizations

• Initial operational support


❌ No funds are used for speculative research

❌ No autonomous or general-purpose AI development

❌ No data resale or behavioral monetization


⸻


4. PRICING & ACCESS MODEL (CORRECTED & LOCKED)


EchoAuth operates under a tiered access model:

• Individual caregivers pay a one-time onboarding fee

• Institutions (schools, clinics, agencies) operate under a subscription agreement

• Parents who can afford may opt into a subscription

• Parents who cannot afford access are supported through government subsidy and charitable programs

• Caregiver staff never pay


No child is excluded due to inability to pay.


⸻

Loan repayment is supported by predictable institutional subscription revenue, supplemented by individual onboarding fees and public or charitable offsets. Because EchoAuth operates under a fixed Memory-Continuity Guard (MCG) that prevents feature creep or scope expansion, operating costs remain bounded and forecastable. Repayment does not rely on speculative growth, advertising, or data monetization.


This creates a stable, conservative repayment profile appropriate for mission-aligned lenders.
