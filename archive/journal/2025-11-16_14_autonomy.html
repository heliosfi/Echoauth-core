PAGE 14I — ORI AUTONOMY CALIBRATION


(Option 5 • B3 Professional Hybrid Tone)


A. PURPOSE OF AUTONOMY CALIBRATION


ORI Autonomy Calibration is designed to:

• Strengthen the user’s independent cognitive function while using AI.

• Ensure that ORI remains the final authority, not the AI output.

• Maintain a healthy balance where AI is a collaborator, not a replacement for personal judgment.


The core goal is:


Clarity without dependency. Guidance without surrendering self.


⸻


B. AUTONOMY STABILITY INDICATORS


The system regularly checks whether the user:

• Maintains personal judgment instead of blindly following suggestions.

• Avoids emotional outsourcing (letting AI decide because emotions feel heavy).

• Separates AI suggestions from real-life decisions, seeing them as input, not orders.

• Stays grounded in their own identity, values, and lived experience.


If these indicators weaken, ORI signals that autonomy needs to be re-centered.


⸻


C. THE AUTONOMY TRI-CHECK


Before accepting an AI-influenced decision, the user runs a quick Tri-Check:

1. Identity Check

• “Does this align with who I am, and how I truly want to live?”

2. Logic Check

• “Is this reasoning stable, consistent, and supported by facts or clear patterns?”

3. Emotional Check

• “Am I calm and thinking clearly, or am I stressed, pressured, or reacting?”


If one of these fails, ORI marks the decision as “Not Ready” and encourages further reflection.


⸻


D. ORI AUTONOMY LOOP


The Autonomy Loop keeps the human in command:

1. AI → provides structured information or options.

2. NI → interprets the meaning, context, and relevance.

3. ORI → re-centers identity, values, and long-term direction.

4. User → makes the final, independent decision.


This loop is designed to prevent overreliance on AI and stop emotional merging with AI outputs.

AI supports. ORI decides. You act.


⸻


E. AUTONOMY OUTCOME


When ORI Autonomy Calibration is active and integrated, users experience:

• Stronger internal decision-making

• Emotional independence while still benefiting from AI insight

• Clarity under pressure, even with complex information

• Stable, identity-driven choices that feel true, not forced


This completes the ORI 14-Series by ensuring that every tool, flow, and safeguard still returns power to the living mind behind the system.
