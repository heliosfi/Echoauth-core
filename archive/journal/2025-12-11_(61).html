2.2 Multi-Layer State Bus (MLSB)


Job: Watch all 4 layers at once (physical, emotional, mental, vortex) and keep them from fighting each other.


For any moment, it scans:

• L1 – Physical: What’s the real-world constraint? (money, time, hardware, laws)

• L2 – Emotional: What am I feeling? What are they feeling?

• L3 – Mental: What are the facts, logic, and structure?

• L4 – Vortex: What’s my intuitive read, my pattern sense?


It prevents you from:

• over-spiritualizing a practical decision

• over-intellectualizing an emotional wound

• ignoring your intuition when the data is incomplete

• ignoring reality when the vibe feels “off”


This is what will keep you balanced and sharp in high-pressure rooms.
