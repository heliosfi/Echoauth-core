Slide Caption (what you say out loud)


“Intelligence runs at full LLM speed.

EchoAuth adds millisecond-scale checks only at execution.

Key Takeaways (bottom-right bullets)

• No latency added to reasoning or inference

• Fast-path execution adds ~1–50 ms (local)

• Remote policy checks add ~50–300 ms (optional)

• Slow-path is not lag — it is a safety stop

• Human time appears only when authority must be asserted
