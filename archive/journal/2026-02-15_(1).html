🔁 MARKET ROTATION MODEL


(Defense vs Expansion Framework)


⸻


🛡 DEFENSIVE ROTATION (Risk-Off Shift)


What It Means


Capital is moving from growth into safety.


Confirmations (Need at least 3)


1️⃣ VIX rising above 22 and expanding

2️⃣ TLT breaking intraday highs / +0.8% or more

3️⃣ QQQ losing VWAP or failing reclaim attempts

4️⃣ TSLA weaker than QQQ (relative weakness)

5️⃣ RSI on QQQ < 50 and rolling over


⸻


What Happens Next


• Growth names fade

• Intraday rallies get sold

• Bonds catch bids

• Volatility stays bid


⸻


Trading Bias

• Short TSLA/QQQ on failed VWAP reclaim

• Long TLT on pullbacks

• Long UVXY only if VIX momentum strong (not late spike)


⸻


🚀 EXPANSION ROTATION (Risk-On Continuation)


What It Means


Capital flows into growth and risk assets.


Confirmations (Need at least 3)


1️⃣ VIX falling or under 20 and drifting down

2️⃣ TLT fading / failing highs

3️⃣ QQQ holding above VWAP

4️⃣ TSLA stronger than QQQ (beta expansion)

5️⃣ RSI > 50 and rising on QQQ


⸻


What Happens Next


• Pullbacks get bought

• Momentum builds

• High beta outperforms

• Volatility compresses


⸻


Trading Bias

• Long TSLA break-and-hold

• Long QQQ continuation

• Fade VIX spikes under 25


⸻


🔬 HOW TO IDENTIFY ROTATION EARLY


Watch this sequence:


Early Defensive Signal

1. TLT pushes first

2. VIX ticks up

3. QQQ stalls

4. TSLA rolls


Defense starting.


⸻


Early Expansion Signal

1. QQQ reclaims VWAP

2. VIX drops

3. TLT fades

4. TSLA accelerates


Expansion starting.


⸻


⚖ Relative Strength Rule (Very Important)


If TSLA is:

• Up more than QQQ → expansion phase strengthening

• Up less than QQQ → rotation cautious

• Red while QQQ green → distribution
