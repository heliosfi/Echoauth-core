ADEWE prevents:

• panic attacks

319.3 — Functional Definition


The engine identifies the physiological “signature” of impending autonomic disruption by analyzing:

1. Facial micro-movements (blink rate, jaw tension, micro-tics)

2. Hand fidget speed

3. Breath pattern irregularity (from audio + movement delta)

4. Head-position micro-tilt (shutdown precursor)

5. Gaze collapse or hyper-focus

6. Micro-pauses in speech or movement

7. Emotional oscillation rate (from E-Thread)

8. Environmental disruptors (noise, light, crowd proximity)

9. Sensory load (color, texture, sound, motion delta)

10. Pre-shutdown stillness (extremely reliable signal)


The engine then calculates the Autonomic Risk Score (ARS) and outputs the predicted dysregulation type.
