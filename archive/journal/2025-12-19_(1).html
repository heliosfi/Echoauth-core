HELIOSFI LLC — ECHOAUTH


Prototype & Non-SBA Funding Overview


Company: HeliosFi LLC

Product: EchoAuth

Stage: Prototype-Ready

Funding Type: Non-SBA (Prototype / Pre-Revenue)


⸻


PROBLEM


Caregivers and institutions rely on authentication systems that assume verbal confirmation or manual credentials (PINs, passwords, cards).

These tools fail for non-verbal and neurodivergent users, creating safety gaps, daily friction, and institutional risk.


⸻


SOLUTION


EchoAuth is a governance-controlled biometric confirmation platform designed for caregiver-supported environments.


Instead of relying on explicit credentials, EchoAuth verifies bounded biometric patterns (gesture, physiological consistency, caregiver-approved states).


The system pauses or refuses action under uncertainty, making it suitable for regulated, high-responsibility settings.


⸻


TARGET USERS

• Caregivers and families

• Autism and neurodivergent support environments

• Education, assisted living, and supervised care settings


EchoAuth is designed for safety and confirmation, not diagnosis or behavioral prediction.
