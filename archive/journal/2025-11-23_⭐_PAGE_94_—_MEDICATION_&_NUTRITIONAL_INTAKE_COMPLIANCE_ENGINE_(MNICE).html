Autistic children often:

• avoid textures, smells, or colors

• cannot describe stomach pain

• eat irregularly

• drink inconsistently

• experience side effects silently

• struggle with dehydration

• can’t verbalize nausea or discomfort

EchoAuth monitors for:


✔ irritability

✔ fatigue

✔ hyperactivity

✔ stomach discomfort

✔ headaches

✔ appetite changes

✔ sleep shifts

✔ heart rate changes
