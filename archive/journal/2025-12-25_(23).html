SniperBot — Auto-Mode Allowance Specification


🔒 Principle (Non-Negotiable)


Automation may execute, but it may never decide.


Auto-Mode is a temporary execution permission, not autonomy.


⸻


1️⃣ Human Arming Requirement (Session-Bound)


Auto-Mode can only activate if ALL are true:

• Manual human confirmation for the session

• Auto-Mode is armed per day, not persistent

• Auto-Mode expires automatically at:

• End of session

• Daily max loss

• Market regime invalidation


No carryover. Ever.


⸻


2️⃣ Market Regime Lock (Hard Gate)


Auto-Mode is allowed only in pre-classified regimes:


✅ Allowed Regimes

• Clean trend day (bull or bear)

• Expansion day with aligned structure

• Post-news continuation (after volatility settles)


❌ Disallowed Regimes

• Chop / range-bound indecision

• Pre-news uncertainty

• First 5–15 minutes of market open

• End-of-day illiquidity

• Any regime transition state


If regime confidence < threshold → Auto-Mode disabled


⸻


3️⃣ Entry Type Whitelist (Strict)


Auto-Mode is allowed only for pre-approved entries:


✅ Allowed Entry Types

• Break & retest (confirmed)

• VWAP flip + hold (with volume)

• Reclaim of key level with structure confirmation


❌ Not Allowed

• Counter-trend

• Mean reversion

• FOMO entries

• News reaction trades

• “It looks strong” logic


Whitelist is explicit.

No dynamic interpretation.


⸻


4️⃣ Risk Constraints (Primary Authority)


Auto-Mode cannot override risk rules.


Hard limits:

• Max risk per trade: ≤ 0.25%–0.5%

• Max trades per day: 1 (2 only if first is partial win)

• Position size auto-calculated from stop distance

• If stop is too wide → trade rejected


Risk breach = Auto-Mode shutdown


⸻


5️⃣ Stop Loss Rules (Mandatory)


Every Auto-Mode trade must have:

• Structure-based stop defined before entry

• No moving stops wider

• Optional:

• Time-stop (no follow-through = exit)

• Break-even after TP1


If stop logic fails to validate → no execution


⸻


6️⃣ Take-Profit Logic (Predefined)


Auto-Mode requires:

• TP ladder defined pre-entry

• TP1: logical structure target

• TP2: extension (ATR or measured move)

• Optional runner with trailing stop


No discretionary chasing.

No adding size.


⸻


7️⃣ Volatility Compatibility Check


Auto-Mode allowed only if:

• Stop distance aligns with day type:

• ~3.5% range day → tighter stops, smaller targets

• ≥6% range day → wider stops, larger targets

• If volatility expands unexpectedly → Auto-Mode pauses


Mismatch = shutdown.


⸻


8️⃣ News & Event Filter (Hard Block)


Auto-Mode is automatically disabled:

• Before major scheduled news (CPI, FOMC, earnings)

• During unscheduled breaking news

• When spreads widen abnormally


Human may re-arm after stabilization only.


⸻


9️⃣ Kill Switches (Always Active)


Auto-Mode shuts off immediately if:

• Max daily loss is hit

• Two consecutive invalidations occur

• Data feed becomes unreliable

• Human presses kill switch

• Governance (MCG) flags uncertainty


No override exists.


⸻


🔟 Logging & Audit (Required)


Every Auto-Mode trade must log:

• Regime classification

• Entry type

• Risk %

• Stop & TP logic

• Execution timestamp

• Outcome


If logging fails → Auto-Mode disabled


⸻


One-Line Lock (Save This)


SniperBot Auto-Mode is permissioned execution under strict regime, risk, and human governance — never autonomous trading.


⸻


Final Recommendation


Auto-Mode should be used rarely:

• On days you already understand

• In structures you’ve already traded manually

• To remove execution error, not to increase frequency
