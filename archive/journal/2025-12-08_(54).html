SECTION 2 — HIPAA COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORK (Healthcare)


Required for: ASD centers, therapists, hospitals, clinicians, insurance partners.


2.1 — Protected Health Information (PHI) Handling


NI-AI Core is designed to not store PHI internally unless specifically configured.


If PHI is processed:

• All PHI is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+)

• All PHI is encrypted at rest (AES-256)

• Access governed by role-based EchoAuth identity profiles

• No PHI is written to logs unless explicitly permitted

• Sessions can be anonymized via hashed identity signatures


2.2 — Minimum Necessary Rule


System enforces:

• Only essential fields are processed

• No unnecessary identifiers collected

• Clinical roles (therapist, clinician) determine allowable data
