🔒 LAYER 1 — IDENTITY KERNEL (IK)


Purpose:

Anchor who is acting before what is processed.


Engineer translation:

A persistent, immutable state container.


Inputs

• biometric signals (optional)

• time-based patterns


Processing

• conflict resolution

Outputs

🔒 LAYER 3 — GOVERNANCE HALO (GH)


Purpose:

Engineer translation:

Policy + safety + routing layer.


Inputs
