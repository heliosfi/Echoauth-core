1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY


EchoAuth™ is a coherence-enforced biometric and emotional-state authentication system designed for ASD children, their parents, and the caregivers who support them.


It solves a critical safety gap:

➡️ Children often cannot verbally express discomfort, fear, or changes in emotional state.

➡️ Parents cannot always monitor every environment (school, daycare, therapy, transportation, community programs).


EchoAuth creates a trusted chain of safety using:

• Non-invasive biometric coherence signals

• Emotional-state detection

• A secure “Chamber” validation system that ensures ONLY trusted adults interact with the child’s profile

• Real-time alerts for parents


This Kiva loan will fund the completion of the MVP, legal protections, and deployment into local ASD caregiver ecosystems.

4. THE PAYMENT PHILOSOPHY (REFINED AS YOU REQUESTED)


Your exact vision, fully clarified:


A) Parents pay the subscription — ONLY if they can afford it


EchoAuth is built to never burden low-income families.


So the model becomes:


✔️ Parents who can afford it

Pay normal monthly subscription.


✔️ Families who are financially struggling

Receive affordable access, backed by:

• Government ASD programs

• Medicaid/waiver systems

• School district contracts

• Subsidized caregiver networks

• Community sponsorship

• OR our kinetic wearable donation pool (explained below)


EchoAuth will ALWAYS have a path for families in need. No child left unprotected.


B) Caregivers DO NOT pay subscription


Most caregivers:

• earn low wages,

• work long hours,

• and are already stretched thin.


So:

✔️ Caregivers pay ONE TIME ONLY for training materials + verification guide.

This ensures:

• No recurring financial burden

• Complete accessibility

• Parental trust

• Professional standardization


Caregiver fee is small, affordable, and often reimbursable by agencies.


C) Government & schools will eventually carry the financial load


Your long-term plan:

• District contracts

• State ASD program contracts

• Medicaid technology reimbursement

• Community mental health partnerships


The burden is NOT on parents.

EchoAuth becomes part of the ASD safety structure, paid by institutions—not struggling families.
