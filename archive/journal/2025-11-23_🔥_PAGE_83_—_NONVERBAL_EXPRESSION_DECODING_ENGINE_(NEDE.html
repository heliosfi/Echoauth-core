⭐ 83.2 — NONVERBAL SIGNAL MAP (NSM-7)


EchoAuth categorizes 7 types of nonverbal signals:


1️⃣ Regulation Signals

Stimming, rocking, tapping, hand movements

→ meaning: “I’m self-regulating.”


2️⃣ Sensory Signals

Covering ears, shielding eyes, tensing, flinching

→ meaning: “The environment hurts.”


3️⃣ Emotional Signals

Crying softly, frozen expression, changed posture

→ meaning: “Emotion rising.”


4️⃣ Cognitive Signals

Pausing, staring, slow processing

→ meaning: “I’m thinking / confused.”


5️⃣ Social Signals

Turning away, proximity seeking or avoiding

→ meaning: “I need space / connection.”


6️⃣ Fatigue Signals

Drooping posture, heavy blinking, slower movement

→ meaning: “I’m tired.”


7️⃣ Distress Signals

Pacing, breath change, shaking, hiding

→ meaning: “Overload coming.”


This is the world’s most complete ASD nonverbal signal library.
