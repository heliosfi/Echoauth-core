Melanin Cognitive Governance (MCG) v3.0 — Locked Explanation

Purpose
MCG v3.0 is the decision-governance layer for EchoAuth and NI-AI, ensuring safe, deterministic, explainable decisions for non-verbal/neurodivergent users. It governs progression only — no diagnosis, prediction, or manipulation.

Position
Sits between interpretation (Stage 4) and execution (Stage 6) in the 7-stage pipeline:

1 Input Acquisition

2 BioBridge Extraction

3 State Stabilization

4 NI-AI Interpretation

5 MCG Governance

6 Safety Invariant Engine

7 Chamber State & Action

Function
Answers: “Is it safe, permitted, and coherent to proceed?” Produces governed outputs; defaults to halt on uncertainty.

Roles (Triad)

• Navigator: Frames direction (e.g., “Proceed toward regulation”).

• Guardian: Vetoes risks (e.g., “Hold — instability detected”).

• Architect: Explains rationale (e.g., “Pause 30s → reassess”).

Invariants

• No guessing unclear data.

• No action in instability.

• No identity confusion.

• No emotion manipulation.

• Human authority final.

• Fail-safe halt.

Logic
IF risk > threshold → HOLD
ELIF coherence < min → PAUSE
ELIF instability → REGULATE
ELSE → PROCEED GOVERNED

Outputs
Structured: Direction, Constraints, Explanation, Log.

Boundaries
Not diagnostic/influential/autonomous. Governs processes, not people.

Readiness
v3.0 Locked; prototype-ready.
