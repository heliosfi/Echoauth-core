(b) Transition Integrity Seal (TIS)


Creates a cryptographic seal with:

(c) Drift-Off Detection Layer (DODL)


If the system accidentally slides out of the state during transition → auto-revert + alert guardian.


(d) Dual-Lock Confirmation (DLC)


Requires BOTH:

• guardian intent

If either fails → transition denied.


(e) Post-Transition Stability Window (PTSW)


After transition, the system holds a 3–10 second safety buffer to confirm stability before continuing.
