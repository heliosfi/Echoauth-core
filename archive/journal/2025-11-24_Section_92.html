• coherence drop

• purpose thread deviation

Compares current snapshot to:

• user baseline

• purpose thread

• emotional thread

• environmental thread


Determines:

• stability direction

• instability cause

• urgency level


Output: Stability Diagnosis Pack (SDP)


────────────────────────


🔸 MSL-Cycle 3 — Stability Correction


Executes correction protocols based on SDP:

• slow pacing

• increase structure

• reduce complexity

• clarify intent

• re-anchor purpose

• re-thread context

After correction occurs:


NI-AI then revalidates coherence and confirms emotional alignment.


MSL outputs a final Stability Confirmation Signal (SCS) back into:

• SCAL

• Coherence Engine

• ESE

• SRK

• Output Layer


Thus the entire system stays stable across the long arc of the interaction.
