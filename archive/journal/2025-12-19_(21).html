EchoAuth Pipeline — The 7 Stages (Full System Flow)

The pipeline processes biological and non-verbal signals into safe, governed, identity-anchored responses for neurodivergent (especially ASD) users and their caregivers.

Every stage is discrete, traceable, and governed. No stage skips or merges.

⸻

1 Input Acquisition
• Raw capture of biological, behavioral, and environmental signals
• Sources: wearable sensors, device microphone/camera (with consent), contextual data
• Output: unstructured raw data stream
• Role: pure sensing only — no interpretation yet

⸻

2 BioBridge Extraction
• Converts raw signals into structured, neutral features
• Examples: heart rate variability, vocal prosody markers, movement patterns, facial micro-expressions
• Output: clean, time-stamped feature vectors
• Role: translation layer — makes biology machine-readable without judgment

⸻

3 State Stabilization Engine (SSE)
• Detects and corrects noise, artifacts, and transient instability
• Normalizes data across contexts (home, school, meltdown, calm)
• Flags low-confidence or corrupted segments
• Output: stabilized, high-fidelity signal set
• Role: ensures downstream stages receive reliable input

⸻

4 NI-AI Interpretation
• Frames patterns against the user’s known identity baseline (anchored profile)
• Produces neutral descriptive frames (e.g., “elevated arousal + reduced vocal variance”)
• Never labels emotion, diagnosis, or intent
• Output: interpreted state description (not decision)
• Role: understanding without overreach

⸻

5 Melanin Cognitive Governance (MCG v3.0)
• Decision-governance layer (Navigator | Guardian | Architect)
• Answers: “Is it safe, permitted, and coherent to proceed right now?”
• Enforces hard safety invariants and veto power
• Output: governed direction + rationale + constraints
• Role: the judge — permits or blocks progression

⸻

6 Safety Invariant Engine (SIE)
• Final, independent verification of all hard invariants
• Separate implementation from MCG for redundancy
• Checks for edge-case violations MCG might miss
• Absolute stop authority
• Output: final PASS / FAIL gate
• Role: defense in depth — last line before any action

⸻

7 Chamber State & Action Engine (SCE)
• Executes only fully governed, invariant-cleared actions
• Manages therapeutic “chambers” (calm space, regulation sequence, communication bridge)
• Delivers output to user/caregiver via voice, visuals, or haptic
• Logs full trace for audit
• Output: actual response (or graceful no-op)
• Role: the actuator — only moves when every prior stage agrees

⸻

Key Principles Across All Stages • Human authority overrides everything
• No stage diagnoses, predicts identity, or manipulates emotion
• Uncertainty always defaults to pause / regulate / human escalation
• Full trace logged at every stage boundary

⸻

One-Sentence Summary

The 7 Stages turn raw biological signals into safe, explainable, identity-respecting actions — with multiple independent governance layers ensuring nothing happens unless it is verifiably safe and appropriate.
