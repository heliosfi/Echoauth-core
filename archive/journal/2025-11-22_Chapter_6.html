6.3 Risk Control Framework


NI-AI’s FI Kernel contains a predictive risk system, not reactive.


Risk Level 1 — Informational Drift


Coherence score dips slightly.

Action: FI Kernel stabilizes emotional + cognitive vectors.


Risk Level 2 — Context Fragmentation


Thread-linking detects broken context chains.

Action: System performs thread reattachment protocol.


Risk Level 3 — Behavior Discrepancy


Emotional & device signatures conflict.

Action: EchoAuth re-verification required.


Risk Level 4 — External Attack Attempt


Indicators:

• repeated failed RMS checks

• abnormal request frequency

• prompt injection patterns

• cross-session anomalies


Action:

• auto-isolation

• request throttling

• FI kernel locks purpose state

Risk Level 5 — Critical Integrity Threat

• signature spoofing

• unauthorized system kernel access


Action:

• lockout of authentication

• alert sent to primary operator
