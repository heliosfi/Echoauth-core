SECTION 379 — MULTI-STATE SYNCHRONIZATION RELAY (MSSR)


(Safety Map Layer 1 — Core Transition Regulator)


379.1 — Technical Field


The MSSR relates to AI safety engineering, specifically to systems for synchronizing multiple internal cognitive states during real-time emotional, behavioral, and environmental transitions.


It ensures all NI-AI safety states move in sync, with zero lag, zero contradiction, and zero cross-thread drift.


⸻


379.2 — Purpose


MSSR prevents:

• State mismatch (e.g., Emotional State: Calm, Environmental State: High-Risk)

• Asynchronous reactions

• Conflicting outputs from different cognitive subsystems

• Safety-degrading delays

• Cross-thread instability during rapid child behavior shifts


It guarantees NI-AI behaves consistently, predictably, and safely at all times.


⸻


379.3 — Functional Definition


The Multi-State Synchronization Relay performs:


(a) State Coherence Polling


Checks all subsystems every 40–90ms to ensure:

• Emotional State Engine (ESE)

• Sensory Load Engine (SLE)

• Prediction Engine (BPE)

• Wearable Data Layer

• Environmental Safety Scanner (ESS)

• Parent Intent Layer

…are aligned in the same transition state.


(b) Transition Lock


Prevents one subsystem from entering a new state unless all other subsystems can follow.


(c) Conflict Suppression


If two states conflict (e.g., “Safe” vs “Overload”), MSSR immediately applies override logic.


(d) Harmonized Transition Curve


Smooths frame-to-frame safety transitions to prevent jarring jumps.


(e) Real-Time Drift Sensor


Detects anomalies:

• latency drift

• sensory overload spikes

• emotional thread desync

• wearable misreads


Then initiates correction.

379.6 — Internal Mechanisms


(a) Multi-State Alignment Matrix (MSAM)


The internal map that decides how each subsystem reacts when a transition is required.


(b) Temporal Lockstep Controller (TLC)

(c) Conflict-Resolution Ladder (CRL)

1. Physical safety

Limits how fast NI-AI can change states to prevent unpredictable behavior.


(e) Safety State Recorder (SSR)


Writes a real-time safety ledger for legal compliance and regulator audits.
