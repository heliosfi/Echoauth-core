Loan Purpose — What This Funding Will Do (Clear & Direct)

I am requesting a Kiva loan to complete the development and launch of EchoAuth, a biometric, safety-focused communication system designed for children with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD), especially non-verbal or low-verbal children.

This loan will allow me to finalize software development, secure licensing, complete testing, and prepare EchoAuth for deployment into homes, daycares, schools, and clinical care environments.


EchoAuth helps caregivers and educators detect distress, verify identity, and maintain safe communication by using a gentle, protective AI system designed specifically for ASD needs.

This loan is essential for bringing EchoAuth into the world and creating meaningful support for children who need it most.


⸻


Why This Matters


Families of ASD children often experience:

• Difficulty understanding their child’s emotional state

• Fear of miscommunication during distress

• High caregiver burnout

• Expensive, inaccessible technological tools


EchoAuth solves this by offering a safe, affordable, easy-to-use companion system that supports emotional, behavioral, and safety needs.


⸻


Who Benefits


1. Low-Income Families

Many ASD families cannot afford expensive technology. EchoAuth includes a low-income affordable tier so no family is denied access for financial reasons.


2. Parents With Means

Parents who can afford it may use a standard subscription that helps sustain the platform.


3. Individual Caregivers (non-institutional)

Nannies, aides, babysitters, respite workers, and family caregivers pay a one-time onboarding fee (not subscription-based), making it accessible and fair.


4. Institutions (Daycares, Schools, Clinical Centers)

Institutions pay the full subscription because they use EchoAuth at scale and may deduct this as a yearly business or educational operating cost.


5. Community-Supported Families

Our upcoming Kinetic Wearables Program, where athletes and everyday users wear energy-harvesting devices that donate micro-amounts, helps subsidize families who cannot pay.


EchoAuth is built on the principle that no child’s ability to communicate or feel safe should depend on their parent’s income.


⸻


Funding Breakdown (Transparent & Responsible)


The Kiva loan will be used strictly for development and launch:


• $5,000 — Software engineering + final safety modules

• $3,000 — Licensing, compliance, and testing

• $2,000 — Cloud infrastructure + security systems

• $1,500 — UX/UI accessibility for ASD users

• $1,500 — Early caregiver training materials

• $2,000 — Pilot program with daycares + ASD families


This allocation ensures EchoAuth is complete, compliant, and ready for real-world use.


⸻


Why I Need This Loan


As a single father, builder, and full-time caregiver myself, I understand the emotional, financial, and logistical strain ASD families go through.

EchoAuth was created because I lived the need personally.


This Kiva loan gives me the last push to finalize the system so I can bring EchoAuth into homes and institutions that urgently need it.


⸻


Ability to Repay


Repayment will come from:

• Parent subscriptions

• Institution subscriptions

• One-time caregiver onboarding fees

• Early licensing agreements

• Pilot program partnerships


These streams provide stable, predictable revenue once EchoAuth launches.


⸻


Social Impact


EchoAuth supports:

• non-verbal and semi-verbal ASD children

• low-income families

• schools and daycares needing safer communication tools

• caregivers under stress

• the larger ASD community seeking clarity, calm, and safety


The goal is to help children feel understood and safe — and help families finally breathe.


EchoAuth is more than technology.

It is a bridge of understanding for families who need it most.
