18.1 Commercial Vision


EchoAuth’s mission is to provide a predictable, secure, ASD-aligned communication and identity tool for:

• autistic children

• non-verbal individuals

• teens and young adults with communication barriers

• schools, clinics, caregivers, and families


The long-term commercial vision:


“Become the national assistive communication and identity platform for neurodivergent individuals, serving schools, clinics, families, and state programs.”
