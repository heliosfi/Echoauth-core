Where speed is intentionally constrained


Only here:


The final step where a proposal would turn into a real-world action.


At that boundary, the system asks one question:


“Is authority resolved right now?”


• If yes → execution proceeds immediately (no delay beyond a gate check)

• If no → execution halts instantly (not slowly)


That halt is not latency.

It’s a binary refusal.
