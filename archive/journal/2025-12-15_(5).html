👋 About Me


My name is Nicholas B. Carty, and I am the founder of Helios F.I LLC, a New York–based small business focused on building caregiver-mediated safety and authentication technology that supports non-verbal communication for children and families.


I started Helios F.I after firsthand experience navigating how stressful and inaccessible many systems can be for families—especially when communication does not rely on speech. My work is rooted in patience, clarity, and safety, with the goal of reducing pressure on caregivers and children while preserving human oversight and dignity.


⸻


💡 About My Business


Helios F.I LLC is developing EchoAuth, a safety-first system designed to help caregivers support non-verbal communication without forcing speech, explanation, or performance.


The system:

• Treats silence and non-participation as valid

• Supports caregiver oversight rather than automation

• Prioritizes safety, predictability, and restraint

• Is built for families, schools, and care settings


This is not a medical or diagnostic product. It is protective infrastructure that helps caregivers respond calmly and consistently in sensitive situations.
