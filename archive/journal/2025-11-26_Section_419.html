419.3 — Functional Definition


The CSRS works by:

1. Capturing the child’s “last stable context frame.”

(Where they were, what they were doing, what the system was guiding.)

2. Detecting environment or task changes.

3. Matching the new environment to the closest “safe re-entry anchor.”

4. Delivering a short stabilizing orientation message.

5. Reactivating the relevant task or safety protocol.
