⭐ 68.2 — THE 7-BEHAVIOR SIGNAL MAP (7-BSM)


EchoAuth recognizes 7 categories of autistic behavior:


🔵 1. Sensory Behavior


covering ears • stimming • hand flapping • pressure seeking


🟢 2. Avoidance Behavior


walking away • refusing • hiding • turning body


🔶 3. Freeze Behavior


silent • still • rigid • staring


🔴 4. Escalation Behavior


yelling • crying • pacing • pushing away


🟣 5. Overload Behavior


hyperventilating • shaking • rapid speech • shutting down


🟡 6. Social Misalignment Behavior


interrupting • saying blunt truths • repetitive questions


⚫ 7. Fatigue Behavior


slouching • zoning out • slow response • low tone


EchoAuth categorizes this instantly.
