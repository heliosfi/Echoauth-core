STAGE 26 — Caregiver Portal: Qualified Caregiver Intake


Lives in: Caregiver Portal


Core feature:

• Intake and verification of ASD-qualified caregivers


Includes:

• ASD experience/training verification

• Background verification (platform standard)

• Agreement to task-scoped authority

• Completion of caregiver onboarding


Does NOT include:

• No decision authority

• No plan activation

• No schedule control


Purpose:

Bring in real help without burning family members.


⸻


STAGE 27 — Caregiver Portal: Task-Scoped Execution


Lives in: Caregiver Portal


Core feature:

• Execution of parent-approved tasks only


Includes:

• Receive alerts/reminders

• Execute assigned tasks

• Log care actions factually


Does NOT include:

• No approvals

• No access expansion

• No interpretation


Purpose:

Hands-on help with zero authority drift.


⸻


STAGE 28 — Caregiver Portal: Caregiver Network Availability


Lives in: Caregiver Portal


Core feature:

• Visibility of available, qualified caregivers (opt-in)


Includes:

• Parent selection of caregivers

• Immediate removal by parent

• Scoped visibility


Does NOT include:

• Automatic matching without parent consent

• Authority escalation


Purpose:

Give parents options, not obligations.

STAGE 29 — Field Intelligence Core: Wearables


Lives in: Field Intelligence Kernel


Core feature:

• Integration of approved wearables


Signals captured (examples):

• Movement patterns

• Routine consistency

• Sleep/wake indicators (device-dependent)

• Safety zones (optional)


System behavior:

• Logs patterns

• Flags change over time


System limits:

• No diagnosis

• No scoring

• No treatment advice


Purpose:

Passive safety and continuity signals.


⸻


STAGE 30 — Field Intelligence Core: Cameras


Lives in: Field Intelligence Kernel


Core feature:

• Parent-approved camera integration


Provides:

• Environmental context

• Safety verification

• Event-linked clips (optional)


Hard limits:

• No facial recognition

• No identity inference

• No emotion analysis

• No continuous surveillance by default


Purpose:

Context, not judgment.


⸻


STAGE 31 — Field Intelligence Kernel: Observation & Logging


Lives in: System Layer (not a human portal)


Core feature:

• Ingests wearable + camera signals


Does:

• Timestamp events

• Log observations

• Flag changes, not conclusions


Does NOT do:

• No decisions

• No alerts without parent rules

• No interpretation


Purpose:

Turn real-world signals into safe records.

STAGE 32 — Parent Control: Sensor & Caregiver Governance


Lives in: Parent Portal


Core feature:

Includes:

• Enable/disable cameras

• Control visibility per role


Purpose:

Keep authority centralized and explicit.
