1. Business Overview


EchoAuth is a next-generation neuro-support and identity authentication system designed for children and adults with communication challenges, including ASD (Autism Spectrum Disorder), non-verbal users, and neurodivergent learners.


The platform uses a structured NI-AI (Natural Intellect + Artificial Intelligence) framework to help users authenticate, express needs, confirm safety, and communicate through a secure cognitive layer.


EchoAuth is engineered as:

• a therapeutic support tool

• a classroom learning assistance system

• a home identity + communication tool

• a clinical companion for ASD specialists


The prototype will be ready for school, clinic, and research pilot programs within months of funding.

2. The Problem EchoAuth Solves


Neurodivergent children rely on fragmented tools for identity, communication, and emotional expression. Current systems:

• lack safety

• lack personalization

• are not built with ASD sensory needs in mind

• cannot authenticate children securely

• cannot read intention or stabilize communication patterns


EchoAuth provides:

• a single, secure identity

• a safe communication channel

• a predictable routine system

• a supportive cognitive layer

• stability for families, teachers, and therapists


This is a high-need, high-impact solution for a market that lacks innovation.
