SECTION 2 — PURPOSE OF THE AGREEMENT


The purpose of this Agreement is to authorize the Pilot Site to participate in a limited deployment of EchoAuth, an assistive communication and identity-support platform designed for autistic and non-verbal individuals.


This Agreement defines:

• permitted uses of the system

• staff responsibilities

• safety and compliance requirements

• data handling practices

• evaluation and reporting terms

• cost and compensation structure (if applicable)


This pilot is for evaluation, research, and refinement purposes only and does not constitute a commercial sale.


⸻


SECTION 3 — SCOPE OF THE PILOT


3.1 Duration


The pilot will operate for:

___ weeks / ___ months, beginning on __________________.


3.2 Number of Participants


Pilot Site agrees to enroll:

___ to ___ ASD users, with guardian consent.


3.3 Supported Environments


EchoAuth may be used in:

• classrooms

• therapy sessions

• private communication settings

• supervised home transitions (if approved)


3.4 Deliverables Provided by HeliosFi

• EchoAuth prototype software

• Pilot Site Setup Guide

• Staff training (virtual or in-person)

• Parent/Caregiver documentation

• Compliance & Safety Manual

• Pilot data collection templates

• Technical support (email + scheduled calls)


⸻


SECTION 4 — RESPONSIBILITIES OF THE PILOT SITE


The Pilot Site agrees to:


4.1 Ensure Appropriate Supervision


Use EchoAuth only under direct supervision of:

• teachers

• clinicians

• therapists

• trained caregivers


4.2 Provide Trained Personnel


At least one staff member must be assigned as Pilot Lead.


4.3 Obtain All Required Consents


Including but not limited to:

• Parent/guardian consent

• FERPA/HIPAA acknowledgements (as applicable)

• User participation approval


4.4 Maintain Safety Requirements


The Pilot Site agrees:

• not to modify or alter the software

• not to expose users to unsafe or overstimulating environments

• to follow the Safety & Compliance Manual


4.5 Participate in Evaluation


Staff agrees to:

• provide weekly feedback forms

• participate in onboarding and debrief meetings

• track communication success metrics


⸻


SECTION 5 — RESPONSIBILITIES OF HELIOSFI (PROVIDER)


HeliosFi LLC will:


5.1 Deliver a fully functional prototype


Including:

• ORI Kernel

• NI-AI Interpreter

• ASD-Safe UI

• Safety Wrapper

• Coherence Engine


5.2 Provide Technical & Training Support

• initial training for staff

• troubleshooting assistance

• guided onboarding sessions


5.3 Ensure Data Safety & Compliance


All system behavior will follow:

• FERPA (education)

• HIPAA (clinical)

• COPPA (children’s data)

• ADA digital accessibility requirements


5.4 Maintain System Reliability


Provider will:

• ensure appropriate uptime

• implement fail-closed behavior

• provide software updates as reasonably required


⸻


SECTION 6 — DATA, PRIVACY & SECURITY


6.1 No Personal Identifiers Collected


EchoAuth does not collect:

• photos

• biometrics

• facial recognition

• audio recordings


6.2 Minimal Data Model


System only stores:

• session identity signatures

• communication selections

• system-use logs

• non-personal meta-data


6.3 Pilot Site Access


Only authorized staff may access data logs.


6.4 Data Storage & Encryption

• All logs encrypted at rest and in transit

• No third-party data sharing

• No cloud uploads unless explicitly approved


6.5 Right to Review & Delete Data


Parents/guardians may request deletion of all data associated with a user at any time.


⸻


SECTION 7 — SAFETY & COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORK


Pilot Site acknowledges the following built-in safety protocols:


7.1 Fail-Closed Behavior


System will refuse unsafe action paths.


7.2 Coherence Checks


All responses pass through:

• narrative consistency

• temporal alignment

• logic gating

• safety filtering


7.3 Guardian Override


Parents/caregivers may override:

• settings

• preferences

• communication categories


7.4 No Autonomous Actions


EchoAuth will never:

• act independently

• transmit messages without supervision

• generate unverified emotional content
