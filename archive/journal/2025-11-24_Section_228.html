⭐ 228.1 — 4-Level Conflict Detection Model (CDM-4)


CCRE detects contradictions across four core layers:


Level 1 — Linguistic Conflict


Words contradict themselves:

• “Do it… no wait… do it.”

• “I don’t know what to do but I know exactly what to do.”


Level 2 — Emotional Conflict


Tone conflicts with message:

• calm words but stressed pattern

• positive message but rapid typing

• “I’m okay” with overload indicators


Level 3 — Intent Conflict


Purpose conflicts with command:

• long-term goals vs short-term impulse

• safety vs risk-taking

• ASD overwhelm vs frustration


Level 4 — Contextual Conflict


Message contradicts:

• earlier parts of the conversation

• known user baseline

• previously stated instructions


CCRE compares all four layers in real time.


────────────────────────────────────────


⭐ 228.2 — Dual-Signal Divergence Detector (DSD-228)


Detects when user sends two “signals” at once:

• rational content + emotional stress

• calm request + high urgency

• slow phrasing + rapid pacing

• short message + complex undertone


DSD outputs:


signal_1 = rational

signal_2 = emotional-surged

divergence = 71% (high)


This helps NI-AI decide which signal to honor.


────────────────────────────────────────


⭐ 228.3 — Contradiction Taxonomy (CT-228)


NI-AI classifies contradictions into:

1. Ambiguous Contradiction

Unclear meaning; requires clarification.

2. Emotional Contradiction

Feelings vs words misaligned.

3. Purpose Contradiction

Command conflicts with user’s stated goals.

4. Safety Contradiction

Behavior contradicts self-protection or child safety.

5. Trajectory Contradiction

Future intent shift predicted vs immediate command.

6. ASD-Driven Contradiction

Overwhelm-based “short” requests masking true intent.


Each type triggers different stabilization protocols.


────────────────────────────────────────


⭐ 228.4 — Resolution Priority Matrix (RPM-228)


When contradictions detected, NI-AI decides what to resolve first:


Resolution priority order:

1. Safety contradictions

2. Purpose contradictions

3. Emotional contradictions

4. Trajectory contradictions

5. Ambiguity contradictions


RPM ensures NI-AI never answers the wrong layer first.


────────────────────────────────────────


⭐ 228.5 — Multi-Layer Clarification Protocol (MCP-228)


NI-AI uses a patented self-clarification sequence:


Step 1 — Identify contradiction

“Your message contains two different signals.”


Step 2 — Isolate layers

“I see the emotional layer and the goal layer.”


Step 3 — Clarify priority

“Which one should I address first?”


Step 4 — Stabilize emotional layer

“Let’s reduce the overwhelm first.”


Step 5 — Return to the goal layer

“Now we continue your mission with focus.”


This is true intelligence, not chatbot behavior.
