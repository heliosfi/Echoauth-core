LOI 1 — School District Pilot Deployment

Subject: Letter of Intent for Child-Safe Pilot of EchoAuth + NI-AI


“We, the undersigned educational institution, express our intent to review and potentially support early pilot deployment of the EchoAuth + NI-AI applied intelligence safety platform for non-verbal ASD child sensory awareness, routine stability, and caregiver-alert translation. This system will be evaluated solely for classroom feasibility, environmental safety compliance, and parent-authority-respecting override behavior. We do not classify emotional data medically, nor do we perform diagnosis, treatment, or AGI-style reasoning within this pilot. LOI 2 — Pediatric Clinic / Therapist Feasibility Review

Subject: Intent to Evaluate Applied Intelligence for Non-Verbal Child Safety


“We, the undersigned pediatric clinical care provider or therapy practice, express our intent to examine the feasibility of the EchoAuth + NI-AI system for safe applied-intelligence deployment during supervised child-care or therapeutic sessions. This review ensures that:

✔ parental authority remains the highest override,

✔ no emotional data is medically labeled,

✔ no diagnostic conclusions are generated,

✔ alerts are purely behavioral/sensory in nature,

✔ offline phone fallback mode is supported where wearables may be banned, and

✔ system behavior is recorded in a parent-controlled, encrypted, minimal-retention audit form.

The goal is to review protective system behavior, caregiver awareness, routine cohesion, and sensor fallback reliability—not to provide medical decisions.”
