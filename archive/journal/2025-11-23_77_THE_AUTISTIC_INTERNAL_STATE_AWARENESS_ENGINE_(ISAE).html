⭐ 77.2 — THE 4 INTERNAL STATE LAYERS (4-ISL)


EchoAuth maps the child’s internal signals into FOUR domains:


1️⃣ Physical State


tired • hungry • thirsty • warm • cold • in pain


2️⃣ Sensory State


too loud • too bright • too fast • too tight • too much


3️⃣ Emotional State


sad • scared • confused • frustrated • overwhelmed


4️⃣ Cognitive State


don’t understand • forgetting • unclear steps • too much info


This makes the invisible visible.

✔ tone

✔ eye movement

✔ breathing

✔ withdrawal

⭐ 77.4 — INTERNAL STATE LABELING SYSTEM (ISLS)


EchoAuth teaches the child how to NAME their state:


Physical:


“I’m tired.”

“I’m hungry.”


Sensory:


“It’s too loud.”

“This tag hurts.”


Emotional:


“I’m scared.”

“I’m sad.”


Cognitive:


“I don’t understand.”

“This is confusing.”


Naming → regulating.
