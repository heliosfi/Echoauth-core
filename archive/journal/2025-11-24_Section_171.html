1. Purpose of MEST-PM


To create a structured safety system that:


✔ Prevents NI-AI from giving harmful or overly complex output in the wrong environment

✔ Controls what NI-AI can say, reveal, recommend, or act on

Each environment is assigned one of four safety tiers.

• More flexible in pacing

• More open with suggestions

✓ Behavioral support

✓ Emotional grounding

✓ Routines, reminders, and step-by-step plans

× No medical diagnosis

× No high-risk decisions without caregiver approval
