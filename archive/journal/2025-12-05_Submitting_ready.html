1. About My Business


EchoAuth™ is a safety and communication system designed for autistic and non-verbal children. It helps parents, caregivers, schools, and clinical centers confirm who is interacting with a child and ensures communication stays safe, calming, and emotionally supportive.


Many families struggle because traditional apps or devices don’t understand neurodivergent needs. EchoAuth is different — it uses a gentle, regulated interaction system to keep children safe, supported, and emotionally understood.


This loan helps me finish the last development steps so I can bring EchoAuth into real homes, daycares, therapy centers, and ASD classrooms.

2. What the Loan Will Be Used For


Your support will allow me to complete the functional, test-ready version of EchoAuth by covering:

• Backend server development & safety integration

• EchoAuth mobile/web interface

• Final testing tools, tokens, and deployment framework

• Security, database, and monitoring setup

• Compliance & early certification steps

• Initial pilot program with families and institutions


This $15,000 loan does not pay salaries or personal expenses — it is strictly for finishing the EchoAuth platform so we can begin serving children and caregivers safely.

3. Why This Matters


Millions of families raising autistic or non-verbal children struggle with:

• communication breakdowns

• unsafe digital interactions

• emotional distress episodes

• inconsistent caregiver support

• lack of visibility into daily progress


EchoAuth gives them a calm, structured, protective system that supports emotional well-being and safe communication for the child.


As a parent—especially one raising a neurodivergent child—I understand the need personally. EchoAuth is not just technology; it is a supportive bridge between families, caregivers, and institutions.

We created a fair, non-burdensome pricing model aligned with your guidance:


A. Families (Parents)
