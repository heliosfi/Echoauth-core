Protocol without modification:

   lane.

   outside scope. Missing prohibitions, broad wording, urgency, low

   tasks, and expected future work do not create permission.

   named lane, allowed file, or allowed action, the required response is
   to stop and report, not to expand.

consolidate away, rank, or bridge these rules.
