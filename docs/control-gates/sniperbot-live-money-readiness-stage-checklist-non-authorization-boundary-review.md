## Purpose

stage checklist for possible future SniperBot live-money readiness
progression while preserving that checklist entries, checklist completion,
stage mapping, prerequisites, dependencies, evidence references, gate
references, cost/timeframe references, and founder-approval relationship
language do not authorize advancement, implementation, runtime, broker
access, order routing, sizing, simulation, paper trading, capital
deployment, evidence acceptance, readiness certification, or live-money
activation.

for each readiness stage. It does not complete any checklist item. It does
not create evidence. It does not accept evidence. It does not validate
evidence. It does not certify readiness. It does not approve advancement.
It does not approve budget or funding. It does not approve implementation
start. It does not authorize simulation runtime, paper trading, broker

## Relationship to Live-Money Readiness Ladder

The live-money readiness ladder defines the required future sequence from
the current governance spine through possible simulation-only readiness,
paper-trading readiness, risk-control readiness, broker-boundary
readiness, order-routing boundary readiness, live-money readiness review,
and explicit live-money founder authorization.

This checklist does not replace the ladder. It maps review categories to
the ladder so that a future reviewer can see what must be checked before a
stage can even be considered. The existence of this checklist does not
advance any ladder stage.

Checklist language must remain subordinate to the ladder. If a checklist
entry appears complete but the ladder requirements are missing, unresolved,
revoked, or superseded, the future stage must remain unadvanced.
